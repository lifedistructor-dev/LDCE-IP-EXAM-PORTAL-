🎯 Topic & Definition 🔴 Very Important

NS (MIS) યોજના એ ભારત સરકારે માસિક વ્યાજ ચૂકવવા માટે બહાર પાડેલી નાની બચત યોજના છે.
NS (MIS) योजना भारत सरकार द्वारा मासिक ब्याज भुगतान हेतु जारी की गई एक लघु बचत योजना है।
The NS (Monthly Income Account) Scheme is a small savings scheme under Sec. 3A of the Govt. Savings Promotion Act, 1873, notified by the Central Govt. to pay monthly interest on a one-time lump sum deposit.
Short title: NS(MIS) Scheme 2019 Commencement: date of Gazette publication Tenure: 5 years Governing Act: Govt. Savings Promotion Act 1873 General Rules: GSPR 2018

✅ Current law update: MIS interest is 7.4% p.a. for the Apr–Jun 2026 quarter, reviewed quarterly. Rate is locked for the account's full 5-year tenure at the time of opening — later revisions apply only to fresh accounts.
  • 🔴 Interest payable monthly on completion of a month from deposit date.
  • 🟠 Unclaimed monthly interest earns NO additional interest (does not compound).
  • 🟡 Interest rounded to nearest ₹1 — ≥50 paisa rounds up, <50 paisa ignored.
  • 🟢 Excess deposit beyond ceiling is refunded immediately + POSA rate interest from deposit date till month before refund.
  • 🟠 If interest due date falls on Sunday/holiday → paid on preceding business day.
  • 🟡 If deposit made on 29/30/31 and that date doesn't exist next month → interest paid on last day of following month (or preceding day if that's a holiday).
Probable Q: Does unclaimed monthly MIS interest earn further interest? Ans: No (para 5(3))

🌳 Decision Tree — Premature Closure 🟠

Before 1 year from opening ➜ ❌ Closure NOT allowed

After 1 year, on/before 3 years ➜ ✂️ Deduct 2% of deposit

After 3 years (before 5-yr maturity) ➜ ✂️ Deduct 1% of deposit

At 5 years (maturity) ➜ ✅ Full deposit + accrued interest, Form-3
🧠 Mnemonic (Gujarati): "1 વર્ષ સુધી બંધ નહીં, 3 વર્ષ સુધી 2% કપાત, પછી 1% કપાત, 5 વર્ષે પૂરું!"
याद रखें: 1=बंद नहीं, 3 तक=2%, उसके बाद=1%, 5=मैच्योरिटी
Trick: "1-No, 3-Two(2%), 5-One(1%)-then Maturity" — numbers 1,3,5 with reducing penalty 2%→1%.
Probable Q: Account closed after 4 years — deduction %? Ans: 1% (closed after expiry of 3 years)

⚰️ Closure on Death of Account Holder 🟡 Conceptual

ખાતાધારકના મૃત્યુ પર, ખાતું GSPR 2018ની જોગવાઈઓ મુજબ બંધ કરી શકાય છે, વ્યાજ સહિત.
खाताधारक की मृत्यु होने पर, खाता GSPR 2018 के प्रावधानों के अनुसार बंद किया जा सकता है, ब्याज सहित।
On death of account holder before maturity, account may be closed per GSPR 2018 provisions, with interest up to the month preceding refund.

🏛️ Important Authorities 🟡

  • Central Government — notifies the scheme under Sec 3A of the 1873 Act; holds power to relax provisions (Para 9).
  • Ministry of Finance (DEA) — revises interest rates quarterly.
  • Accounts Office (Post Office) — opens/closes accounts, pays interest, refunds excess deposits.
  • National Savings Institute (NSI) — administrative/technical body under Ministry of Finance.

⚠️ Important Penalties / Deductions 🟠

SituationDeduction
Premature closure between 1–3 years2% of deposit
Premature closure after 3 years (before maturity)1% of deposit
Closure before 1 yearNot permitted at all

🚩 Important Exceptions 🟠

  • Deposit exceeding ceiling → refunded immediately, not forfeited, but earns only POSA rate (not MIS rate) for the excess period.
  • Death before maturity → account closed under GSPR 2018 provisions (overrides normal premature-closure penalty structure).
  • Interest due date on Sunday/holiday → shifts to preceding business day (not following day).
  • Central Govt. may relax any provision causing undue hardship (Para 9) — exception to rigid rule application.
