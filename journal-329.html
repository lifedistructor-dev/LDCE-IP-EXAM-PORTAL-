🧠 Quick Memory Trick

Golden Rules: Personal A/c — Debit the receiver, Credit the giver. Real A/c — Debit what comes in, Credit what goes out. Nominal A/c — Debit expenses/losses, Credit incomes/gains.

🔵 Discount Allowed is an expense (Dr.) when we receive payment; Discount Received is an income (Cr.) when we make payment.

🟢 GST: Input CGST/SGST/IGST on purchases is an asset (Dr.); Output CGST/SGST/IGST on sales is a liability (Cr.) until set off.
