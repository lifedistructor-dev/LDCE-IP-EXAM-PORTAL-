Memorandum: Formal covering letter proposing the inquiry, directs official to submit written defense within 15 days
Schedule I — Articles of Charge: Precise statement of what rule/conduct was violated (the "charge" itself)
Schedule II — Statement of Imputations: Detailed factual narrative + evidence trail supporting each Article
Schedule III — List of Documents: Every physical/digital document relied upon to prove the charge
Schedule IV — List of Witnesses: Names + role of every person who will depose in the inquiry

SCHEDULE - I
Statement of Articles of Charge framed against Shri A.B. Kumar, PA, Sabarmati SO
Article of Charge - I
That the said Shri A.B. Kumar while functioning as Postal Assistant at Sabarmati SO during the period from 10.01.2026 to 25.02.2026, committed gross financial irregularity by fabricating fraudulent withdrawal entries in Post Office Savings Bank (POSB) Account Number 105432XX. He forged the signatures of the depositor and unauthorizedly withdrew an amount of ₹85,000/- for personal utilization.
By his above acts, Shri A.B. Kumar failed to maintain absolute integrity and devotion to duty, thereby violating Rule 3(1)(i) and Rule 3(1)(ii) of the CCS (Conduct) Rules, 1964.
Article of Charge - II
That during the aforesaid period, the said Shri A.B. Kumar intentionally suppressed the daily closure balances and generated false Finacle transaction logs to prevent detection by the supervisory staff.
By his deliberate acts, the official acted in a manner highly unbecoming of a Government Servant, violating Rule 3(1)(iii) of the CCS (Conduct) Rules, 1964.
(K.P. Sharma)
Disciplinary Authority

अपने उपरोक्त कृत्यों से, श्री ए.बी. कुमार पूर्ण सत्यनिष्ठा और कर्तव्य के प्रति समर्पण बनाए रखने में विफल रहे, जिससे सीसीएस (आचरण) नियम, 1964 के नियम 3(1)(i) और नियम 3(1)(ii) का उल्लंघन हुआ।
आरोप का लेख (Article) - II
यह कि उक्त अवधि के दौरान, उक्त श्री ए.बी. कुमार ने पर्यवेक्षी कर्मचारियों द्वारा पकड़े जाने से बचने के लिए जानबूझकर दैनिक क्लोजर बैलेंस को छुपाया और गलत फिनाकल (Finacle) लेनदेन लॉग जेनरेट किए।
अपने इस कृत्य से उक्त कर्मचारी ने एक सरकारी सेवक के लिए अत्यधिक अनुचित व्यवहार किया, जिससे सीसीएस (आचरण) नियम, 1964 के नियम 3(1)(iii) का उल्लंघन हुआ।
