📘 CHAPTER 1: APPLICABILITY & ELIGIBILITY

1️⃣ Applicability of LTC Rules (Rule 1(3)&(4)) — प्रयोज्यता

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

LTC नियम सिविल सेवा कर्मचारियों, प्रतिनियुक्ति पर आए राज्य कर्मचारियों, संविदा नियुक्ति एवं पुनः नियोजित कर्मचारियों पर लागू होते हैं।

LTC Rules apply to civil service employees, State Govt employees on deputation to Centre, contract appointees, and re-employed pensioners.

✅ Applicable❌ NOT Applicable
Civil services/posts (incl. civilian Defence staff)Not-whole-time Govt servants
State Govt employees on deputation to CentreCasual/daily-rated employees
Contract appointeesPersons paid from contingencies
Re-employed after retirement🔴 Railway servants* (special dispensation)
-Armed Forces members
-Local recruits in Indian Missions abroad
-Persons eligible for any other travel concession

⚠️ Important Exceptions

  • *Railway employees CAN avail "All India LTC" once in a block of 4 years under CCS(LTC) Rules 1988, subject to conditions [OM dt. 27.03.2018].
  • Autonomous body officers on deputation to Centre → treated as temporary Govt servants for LTC.
  • Industrial/work-charged staff with regular leave entitlement → eligible; their first block was 1960-61.

3️⃣ Scope & Definitions — "Family" (Rules 3 & 4) — परिवार की परिभाषा

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

LTC सरकारी कर्मचारी स्वयं तथा उसके परिवार को कवर करता है। "परिवार" में पति/पत्नी, 2 जीवित आश्रित अविवाहित बच्चे, आश्रित माता-पिता आदि शामिल हैं।

LTC covers the Government servant and family. "Family" includes spouse, two surviving dependent unmarried children, dependent parents, and certain other relatives.

Included in FamilyCondition
SpouseOnly 1 wife (2nd allowed only if Govt-permitted 2nd marriage)
2 surviving unmarried children/stepchildrenWholly dependent (residing or not)
Divorced/abandoned/separated/widowed daughtersResiding with + wholly dependent
Parents/step-parentsWholly dependent (residing or not)
Unmarried minor brothers, unmarried/divorced/widowed sistersResiding with + dependent + parents not alive/dependent

⚠️ Important Exceptions (2-child restriction)

  • Applies from 20.10.1997 — does NOT apply to those who already had >2 children before this date.
  • Children born WITHIN 1 year of 20.10.1997 — excluded from restriction.
  • 2nd child birth results in multiple births (twins etc.) — all covered.
  • Children of divorced/widowed SISTERS are NOT included in "family".

🟡 Conceptual Notes

  • Husband & wife (both Govt servants) — dependency condition NOT applicable; treated as one unit.
  • Female Govt servant marrying while on hometown LTC → husband's onward journey from hometown to HQ also covered; new wife's journey too.
  • LTC to hometown/India allowed during leave preparatory to retirement, if return journey completes before leave ends.
🧠 "2 Bacche 1997 ke baad, Twins/Pehle-se-jyada Exempt" | "Pati-Patni = 1 Unit, Dependency Nahi Chahiye"

6️⃣ Admissibility of LTC (Rule 7) — LTC की प्रवेश्यता

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

LTC श्रेणी (i) व (iii) [सिविल सेवा व संविदा] के कर्मचारियों को यात्रा दिनांक तक 1 वर्ष सतत सेवा पूर्ण होने पर देय है। यह किसी भी अवकाश (आकस्मिक अवकाश सहित) के दौरान देय है।

Admissible to categories (i) & (iii) only if 1 year continuous service completed on date of journey. Admissible during any leave, including casual and special casual leave.

📖 Important Rules / Notes

  • Not admissible for journeys during weekend holidays without any leave.
  • LTC also admissible during Child Care Leave (CCL) and Study Leave.
  • Technical resignation within 8 years + rejoining Govt post → LTC entitlement carried forward (else treated as fresh recruit).
  • Hometown declared outside India → LTC admissible but with LIMITED Government assistance.
  • Suspended Govt servant → CANNOT avail LTC himself; family members ARE entitled.
  • Both spouses (Govt servants, residing together) → CAN claim LTC separately (subject to conditions).

दो प्रकार: (a) गृहनगर LTC — हर 2 वर्ष के ब्लॉक में एक बार; (b) भारत में कहीं भी LTC — हर 4 वर्ष के ब्लॉक में एक बार।

Two types: (a) Hometown LTC — once in a block of 2 calendar years; (b) Any Place in India LTC — once in a block of 4 calendar years.

TypeFrequencyDistance

Block 2022-2025 (4-year block); sub-blocks: 2022-2023 and 2024-2025 (2-year blocks each).

⚠️ Important Rule

Any-Place-in-India LTC availed = adjusted AGAINST the Hometown LTC available at time of journey commencement (i.e. it replaces one hometown LTC turn).

8️⃣ Counting Against Blocks & Carry Over (Rules 9 & 10) — ब्लॉक गणना एवं आगे ले जाना

🔴 Very Important
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

यात्रा उस ब्लॉक में गिनी जाती है जिसमें बाहर जाने वाली यात्रा (outward journey) शुरू हुई, भले ही वापसी यात्रा ब्लॉक समाप्त होने के बाद हो। अप्रयुक्त LTC अगले ब्लॉक के पहले वर्ष में ले जाई जा सकती है।

LTC is counted against the block in which the OUTWARD journey commenced, even if return journey is after block expiry. Unavailed LTC can be carried forward into the FIRST YEAR of the next block only.

⚠️ Important Rule — Carry Forward Chain

To carry forward "Any Place in India" LTC (4-yr block), the Govt servant must have ALSO carried forward the Hometown LTC of the 2nd 2-year sub-block within that 4-year block.

🔟 Reimbursement of LTC Claim (Rule 13) — LTC दावे की प्रतिपूर्ति

प्रतिपूर्ति केवल शॉर्टेस्ट डायरेक्ट रूट पर point-to-point through ticket के आधार पर होगी; स्थानीय यात्रा एवं आकस्मिक व्यय शामिल नहीं।

Reimbursement only for shortest direct route, point-to-point, through ticket. Incidental expenses and local journey costs NOT covered.

🗓️ Key Time Limits (Claim Processing)

⚠️ Important Rules

  • PNR number mandatory for LTC claims (post-computerized railway booking).
  • Service Tax/Education Cess/similar levies on travel — reimbursable.
  • Travel agent service charges (for booking) — NOT reimbursable.
  • Air tickets purchasable ONLY via 3 Authorized Travel Agents: BLCL, Ashok Travels & Tours, IRCTC.
  • Children aged <5 yrs — NO air fare reimbursement if parent not air-entitled; children 5-12 yrs — actual rail fare reimbursed.
  • Private/personal transport beyond public transport availability — reimbursed up to max 200 km (100 km each side) on self-certification.

1️⃣1️⃣ Forfeiture of Claim (Rule 14) — दावे का समापहरण

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

यदि अग्रिम नहीं लिया गया हो, तो वापसी यात्रा पूर्ण होने के 3 महीने के भीतर दावा प्रस्तुत करना अनिवार्य है, अन्यथा दावा समाप्त (forfeit) हो जाएगा — कोई छूट नहीं।

If no advance drawn, claim must be submitted within 3 months of completing return journey. Failure → claim is FORFEITED — no relaxation permissible.

Within 3 months — normal deadline (no advance drawn)
Up to 6 months — Ministry/Dept with FA concurrence may admit claim (no advance drawn), without reference to DoPT
Within 1 month — if advance WAS drawn, claim must be submitted after return journey completion

⚠️ Important Rule

🔴 No relaxation permissible beyond stated limits for the 3-month forfeiture rule itself (only the 3→6 month extension is allowed with FA concurrence).

🧠 "3 Mahine — No Advance; 1 Mahina — Advance Liya Tha"
❓ Probable Q: Within what time must an LTC claim be submitted if no advance was drawn? What if an advance was drawn? / अग्रिम न लेने पर दावा कितने समय में देना होगा?

1️⃣2️⃣ Grant of Advance & Adjustment (Rule 15) — अग्रिम की स्वीकृति

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

LTC अग्रिम अनुमानित यात्रा व्यय (आने-जाने) का अधिकतम 4/5वां भाग तक स्वीकृत किया जा सकता है।

Advance limited to 4/5th (80%) of the estimated round-trip cost.

📖 Key Conditions

  • Family travelling separately → advance may be drawn separately.
  • Advance for BOTH forward & return journey together — only if leave/absence period ≤ 90 days (3 months). If exceeded, advance for OUTWARD journey only.
  • If 90-day limit exceeded AFTER advance drawn for both journeys → half the advance must be refunded immediately.
  • 🔴 Outward journey must commence within 30 days of advance grant, else FULL refund required.
  • If reservation made 60 days in advance → tickets must be produced within 10 days of drawal of advance.
SituationConsequence
Advance drawn, journey not started in 30 daysFull refund
Absence exceeds 90 days after both-journey advanceHalf refund immediately
Claim not submitted within 1 month (advance drawn)Refund entire advance lump sum (no instalments)
🧠 "4/5th Advance, 30 din mein Yatra Shuru, 90 din Limit"

धोखाधड़ी वाले LTC दावे पर अनुशासनिक कार्यवाही होने पर, कार्यवाही पूर्ण होने तक LTC नहीं मिलेगा। दंड लगने पर 2 अतिरिक्त सेट भी रोके जाएंगे।

If disciplinary proceedings initiated for fraudulent LTC claim, LTC is withheld till finalization. If penalty (under CCS-CCA Rule 11) is imposed, next TWO sets of LTC also withheld — in addition to sets already withheld.

1️⃣5️⃣ Interpretation, Power to Relax & Saving (Rules 17, 18, 19) — व्याख्या एवं शिथिलीकरण शक्ति

🟢 Quick Revision
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📖 Rule 17 — Interpretation

Any doubt regarding provisions → referred to Department of Personnel & Training (DoPT) for decision.

📖 Rule 18 — Power to Relax

Ministry/Department may relax rule requirements in cases of undue hardship, by recorded written order — BUT ONLY with DoPT's concurrence.

📖 Rule 19 — Saving

Existing instructions not contrary to these rules, and matters not specifically covered, continue in force until amended/modified/cancelled.

📌 Fresh Recruits — Special LTC Facility

नए भर्ती कर्मचारी 4 वर्ष के ब्लॉक में परिवार सहित 3 बार गृहनगर तथा चौथे अवसर पर भारत में कहीं भी यात्रा कर सकते हैं — यह सुविधा केवल पहले 2 ब्लॉक तक।

Fresh recruits may travel to hometown with family on 3 occasions in a 4-year block, and Any Place in India on the 4th occasion — available only for first 2 blocks of 4 years after joining.

📖 Special Dispensation — NE/Ladakh/J&K/A&N/Lakshadweep

  • Employees posted in these regions who leave family behind (without availing transfer TA) get special LTC options.
  • "Emergency Passage Concession" — 2 additional occasions during ENTIRE career.
  • Air travel scheme to NER/J&K/Ladakh/A&N extended periodically (e.g. 26.09.2022 to 25.09.2024).

📖 Key Miscellaneous Rules

  • Officer deputed for training → place of training becomes new HQ for LTC purposes too.
  • Deputationist to PSU → option to choose PSU's LTC OR Central Govt Rules, whichever more favourable; fresh option allowed if PSU rules revised later.
  • Deputation to PSU top-level post (where LTC unavailable) → still eligible for Central Govt-pattern LTC if incorporated in foreign service order.
  • Handicapped single Govt servant → LTC facility extended to an escort accompanying them.
  • Tours by ITDC/STDC/IRCTC → qualify for LTC purposes (subject to conditions).
  • Flexi-fare (dynamic fare) in Rajdhani/Shatabdi/Duronto — admissible for LTC (not for non-entitled employees flying & claiming train-class reimbursement).
