Chapter I — Electronic / Foreign / Value Payable Money Orders▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

🔴eMO is a web-based rapid money transfer service — an order issued by a post office for payment of a sum of money electronically to a named payee. eMO एक वेब-आधारित तीव्र धन हस्तांतरण सेवा है जिसके द्वारा डाकघर द्वारा इलेक्ट्रॉनिक रूप से भुगतान किया जाता है।

VPMO (Value Payable MO): System to collect payment for articles (goods/bills/RRs) at the time of delivery, on behalf of traders/senders.

FMO / IFS-eIMO: Foreign Money Order (paper-based, with Nepal & Bhutan) vs IFS-eIMO (electronic international remittance via UPU's International Financial System, currently with France's La Poste & UAE).

📌 Important Rules & Limits

⚖️ Key Sections/Rules Cited

  • Rules in Post Office Guide Part-I; procedure in Postal Manual Vol-VI Part-II
  • FHB Vol-I Rule 110–113 — acceptance of cheques for eMO
  • Section 44, Indian Post Office Act 1898 — attachment of eMO by civil court (Rule 65, PM VI Part-II procedure)

🏛️ Important Authorities

  • General Manager/Director (PAF) — 23 circle-wise Postal Accounts Offices (list includes Ahmedabad, Delhi, Kolkata, Mumbai (CPMG functions), Chennai etc.)
  • CF Section, IR&GB Division, Postal Directorate — nodal for FMO/IFS-eIMO rules
  • DAA Section — settles FMO/IFS-eIMO accounts (Value Statement / Repayment Statement)
  • IFS Helpdesk — functional at CEPT Mysore

⏱️ Important Time Limits

T+3 day — Check eMO issue value/commission vs CGA/PFMS report
5th of following month — Detail check & rectification before monthly consolidation
15th April — Nodal PAO transfers unpaid eMOs of previous FY as "forfeited" to revenue
End of month following month of account — Random debit-check of eMO payment completed
31st August — Consolidated eMO statistics sent to PA Wing after March Supplementary
20th September — Annual eMO Report sent by PA wing to Statistics Section

🚫 Exceptions

  • Commission NOT charged on Service eMO or specially exempted eMOs (by DG Posts notification)
  • Void MO CAN still be paid after currency expiry if payment is otherwise possible (subject to release procedure)
  • No MO re-issue to heir of deceased remitter/payee unless claim acknowledged by competent authority

Chapter II — Post Office Cash Certificates (NSC/KVP)▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

Government of India instituted National Savings Certificates of various denominations sold at Post Offices doing SB business, redeemable per Post Office Guide rules. छोटी बचत को बढ़ावा देने हेतु सरकार द्वारा जारी प्रमाणपत्र योजनाएँ।

🔴 Sale of pre-printed Cash Certificates stopped from 01.07.2016 in all CBS/non-CBS offices; pass books now issued on opening NSC/KVP accounts.

📌 Important Rules

SchemeMinimum / Multiple
NSC VIII IssueMin ₹1,000, multiples of ₹100
KVPMin ₹1,000, multiples of ₹1,000

Governing manual: PO SB Manual Volume-II. Main check register: Stock & Issue Register (SIR) Form D.G.(PA)-323 (now largely replaced by PATRAM software in CC Section).

🟠 Freeze code: "SCWFR" applied centrally every 30th September; published on indiapost.gov.in in first week of October. Post office must attempt contact at least twice within 60 days of notice display.

🏛️ Authorities & Process for Closure of Unclaimed A/c

  • Closure permitted only at Head Post Offices
  • Requires fresh self-attested KYC of account holder + 2 witnesses
  • Head Postmaster endorses "CLOSURE ALLOWED" with signature + designation stamp
  • Two different HO Supervisors — one unfreezes, another verifies in Finacle CBS (segregation of duties)

⚖️ Key Head of Accounts

  • Lapsed certificate balances (after 3 FY) → Minor Head "0075-00-101 Unclaimed Deposits" (GL 8007500010)
  • Subsequent claim payments → debited to "0075-900 Deduct Refunds" (GL 8007500090)
  • Duplicate certificate issue — Rule 567(8) & 573(1), P&T Manual Vol-VI Part-II

Chapter III — Indian Postal Orders (IPO) & e-IPO▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

IPO is a means of sending/receiving small amounts of money across India, sold and paid at all Head/Sub/GPOs. भारतीय पोस्टल ऑर्डर — छोटी राशि भेजने का सरल माध्यम।

e-IPO: Electronic purchase of IPO via e-Post Office Portal (epostoffice.gov.in) using debit/credit card — mainly to enable RTI Act 2005 fee payment for citizens in India & abroad.

📌 Important Rules

  • Stock indent: HO stock fixed by Divisional Head (6-month requirement); SO stock fixed by Head Postmaster (1-month requirement)
  • Supply source: Security Printing Press, Hyderabad – 500063 (annual indent for 12 months' requirement from following January)
  • Governing manual: Postal Manual Volume-VI Part-II
  • Lost IPO after payment: certificate of payment under Rule 402/24, Postal Manual Vol-VI Part-I

⏱️ Important Time Limits

ItemTime Limit
IPO sold-figure vs inventory check reportBy 15th of following month
Extra commission on order paid after6 months (else charged separately)
AAO review of 2% highest-denomination paid ordersOngoing/monthly
Unclaimed IPO preservation before transfer12 months from month of issue
IPO head kept open (then lapses)2 financial years → "Forfeited Indian Postal Orders"
Paid IPO record destructionApril of 2nd year following year of payment
🧠 Mnemonic: IPO = "2 साल में गायब" (2 FY then lapses) vs eMO = "3 साल में गायब" (3 FY) — याद रखें eMO का समय IPO से ज़्यादा है।

Chapter VI — Savings Bank (Para 6.01 to 6.13)▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

Department of Posts manages Post Office Savings Accounts as an agent of the Government of India, receiving deposits & paying withdrawals per rules in the Post Office SB Manual. डाकघर बचत बैंक — भारत सरकार की ओर से डाक विभाग द्वारा संचालित।

🔴 All POSA transactions run on Core Banking Solution (CBS) — Finacle app — pushed to DOP ERP (CSI F&A) on T+1 basis, and reconciled with e-Lekha (PFMS).

Chapter VIII — Customs Duty▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

India Post acts as an agent of the Customs Department for clearance and realisation of Customs Duty on dutiable inward foreign mail articles/parcels. डाक विभाग सीमा शुल्क विभाग के एजेंट के रूप में कार्य करता है।

Governing rules: Chapter 1, Posts & Telegraphs Manual Vol-VI Part-I; procedure in Foreign Post Manuals.

🏛️ Offices of Exchange

🔴 Main: Mumbai, Kolkata, Delhi, Chennai; Sub-exchange: Jaipur, Ahmedabad, Cochin, Bangalore.

💰 Commission Rate

ItemRate
Commission on Customs Duty realised10% of total duty realised
Commission on net sale of abandoned parcels10% of net sale proceeds
🧠 Mnemonic: "Customs = 10% Commission Club" — दोनों ही जगह (duty realised व abandoned parcel sale) दर 10% ही है — भ्रमित न हों।

⚖️ Key Heads of Account

  • Postal Deposits – short debit/excess recovery on Customs Duty (Form ATC-80/ATC-64)

🟢 No GST liability arises separately on Customs Duty — the GL covers basic price + Basic Customs Duty + IGST + appropriate postage (₹30 or ₹100).

Chapter IX — Goods & Services Tax (GST) — DOP Perspective▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

GST is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based indirect tax on supply of goods & services, subsuming most former indirect taxes. वस्तु एवं सेवा कर — गंतव्य आधारित बहु-स्तरीय अप्रत्यक्ष कर।

🔀 Comparison Chart: Types of GST

TypeGoverning ActRevenue Goes To
CGSTCentral GST Act 2016 (2017)Central Government
SGST (UGST in UTs)State GST Act 2016 (2017)State Government / UT
IGSTIntegrated GST Act 2016 (2017)Shared between Centre & State (inter-state, import/export)
🧠 Mnemonic: "Centre gets CGST, State gets SGST, Inter-state gets IGST (shared)."

⚖️ Key Sections

  • 🔴 Sections 22–24, GST Act (read with Rule 12) — mandatory DDO registration under GSTIN
  • Sections 24, 25(6), 51 — DDOs dealing under third-party payments must register
  • Section 51 — TDS deductor registers without threshold limit; may use TAN if no PAN
  • Section 2(57), GST Rules & Section 2(1)(d), IGST Act — definition of Input Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Section 140 — Transitional credit carry-forward (post-GST rollout, June 30, 2017 cut-off)
  • Section 31 — Invoice requirements for ITC claim
  • Section 50(1) — Interest on wrongly availed ITC

Chapter X — India Post Passenger Reservation System (PRS)▶

📖 Definition | परिभाषा

Railway Reservation tickets sold at selected Post Offices (rural/semi-rural areas without rail-head), in association with Ministry of Railways (MOR), under a Memorandum of Understanding dated 31 July 2007. डाकघरों के माध्यम से रेलवे आरक्षण टिकट सेवा।

🟠 Presently available at 333 Post Offices across India.

💰 Incentive to Postal Staff

RoleRate per Ticket
Booking Assistant at PRS counter₹3

🧠 Mnemonic: "3 for Booking, 1 for Boss" — Booking Assistant ₹3, Supervisor ₹1 per ticket.

📜 MoU Key Points (31 July 2007)

  • Signed between MOR (Additional Member Commercial) and India Post (CGM, BD&M Directorate)
  • MOR provides hardware/communication equipment (cost borne by MOR); India Post provides accommodation & staff (at own cost)
  • 🔴 MoU valid for 5 years, reviewed annually, renewable
  • India Post fully liable/indemnifies Railways for losses due to its own act/omission
