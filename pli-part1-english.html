🎯 Topic & Scope 🔴 Very Important

પોસ્ટ ઓફિસ જીવન વીમા નિયમો, 2011 PLI (સરકારી/PSU કર્મચારીઓ માટે) અને RPLI (ગ્રામીણ રહેવાસીઓ માટે) ને સંચાલિત કરે છે.
डाकघर जीवन बीमा नियम, 2011 PLI (सरकारी/PSU कर्मचारियों के लिए) और RPLI (ग्रामीण निवासियों के लिए) को नियंत्रित करते हैं।
Post Office Life Insurance Rules, 2011 govern PLI (for Govt./PSU employees) and RPLI (for rural residents).
Extent: Whole of India Accounts: Director PLI, Kolkata Age eligibility: 19–55 years Max Sum Assured (PLI): ₹50 lakh

📖 Key Definitions 🔴 High-Yield

Free Look Period: પોલિસી મળ્યાના 15 દિવસમાં રદ કરવાની વિનંતી કરી શકાય.
Free Look Period: पॉलिसी मिलने के 15 दिनों के भीतर रद्द करने का अनुरोध किया जा सकता है।
Free Look Period: 15 days from policy delivery — insurant may request cancellation.
Tap to expand key defined terms ▾
  • Auto Paid-up: 36+ premiums paid, 3+ years — policy matures on original date even if holder discontinues.
  • Commutation: Any contract change EXCEPT maturity date.
  • Conversion: Change AFFECTING maturity date (e.g., WLA → EA).
  • CWLA: Pays at 80 or death; convertible to EA at end of 5 years.
  • Date of Commencement of Risk: Date proposal is accepted — no claim before this.
  • Lapsing: Policy >3 years, unpaid premium >12 months.
  • Period of Grace: Till last day of the due month.
  • Surrender Value: Payable only if 36 premiums paid AND 36 months duration.
  • Void: Policy <3 years, premium unpaid beyond due date/grace.
  • WLA: Pays at age 80 or death, whichever earlier.
Probable Q: Free Look Period duration? Ans: 15 days

🔀 Eligibility Conditions (Rule 6) 🔴 High-Yield

ઉંમર 19 થી 55 વર્ષની વચ્ચે હોવી જોઈએ.
आयु 19 से 55 वर्ष के बीच होनी चाहिए।
Age must be 19–55 years on next birthday on date of proposal (except AEA, Joint Life, Children Policy).
Central/State Govt. employees, GDS, Nationalized Banks, Defence/Para-military
CSIR, Medical/Dental/Nursing/Pharmacy Council employees
Industrial/Work-charged employees (Dept. of Posts/Telecom)
Autonomous body, Joint Venture (Govt./PSU ≥10%) employees
Cooperative Society, Deemed University, Scheduled Commercial Bank employees
Private educational institution staff; Professionals (Doctors, Engineers, CAs, Lawyers)
Listed company (NSE/BSE) employees with PF/Gratuity coverage
⚠️ Exam Trap: Age >50, SA >₹5 lakh: min term 7 years; age 54: min term 6 years.
Probable Q: General age eligibility for PLI? Ans: 19–55 years

🧩 Types of Plans 🔴 High-Yield

Anticipated Endowment Assurance (AEA) — સુમંગલ ▾
01-02-1984 થી શરૂ. બે term: 15/20-વર્ષ. Entry age 19; મહત્તમ 45/40. Sum assured 4 હપ્તામાં.
Introduced 1984. Terms: 15yr/20yr. Entry age 19; max age 45/40. SA in 4 instalments. No surrender/loan/conversion.
Rural Postal Life Insurance (RPLI) ▾
24-03-1995 થી શરૂ. ફક્ત ગ્રામીણ ભારતીય રહેવાસીઓ માટે. ઉંમર 19-55.
Introduced 1995. For rural Indian residents only. Age 19-55.
Ten Year Rural PLI — ગ્રામ પ્રિય ▾
10-વર્ષનો term. ઉંમર 19-45. 2 survival હપ્તા + maturity પર બાકી + bonus.
10-year term only. Age 19-45. 2 survival instalments + remainder at maturity. No surrender/loan/conversion.
Joint Life Assurance / યુગલ સુરક્ષા ▾
01-08-1997 થી શરૂ. Term 5-20 વર્ષ. Entry age 21-45; maturity પર spouse ≤60 વર્ષ.
Introduced 1997. Term 5-20 years. Entry age 21-45; at maturity older spouse ≤60. Both need medical exam.
Children Policy — બાલ જીવન બીમા ▾
20-01-2006 થી શરૂ. મહત્તમ 2 બાળકો; child age 5-20; parent <45. Min SA ₹20,000.
Introduced 2006. Max 2 children; child age 5-20; parent age <45. Max PLI ₹3L / RPLI ₹1L (or parent's SA, lower).
Probable Q: AEA has how many terms and survival instalments? Ans: 2 terms, 4 instalments

🌳 Decision Tree — Non-Medical Claim Before Maturity (Rule 16) 🔴 High-Yield

Death < 1 year ➜ 35% of SA + bonus

Death 1–2 years ➜ 60% of SA + bonus

Death 2–3 years ➜ 90% of SA + bonus

Death after 3 years ➜ Full SA + bonus
🧠 Mnemonic: "35-60-90-100" — climbing tiers each year.
Probable Q: Death occurs 1.5 years after acceptance — payout %? Ans: 60%

✍️ Assignment & Nomination (Rule 36) 🟠

પોલિસી "valuable consideration" માટે અથવા "gift" તરીકે assign કરી શકાય.
पॉलिसी "valuable consideration" या "उपहार" के रूप में assign की जा सकती है।
Policy assignable for valuable consideration OR as a gift.
  • 🔴 Assignment via endorsement OR separate deed — dated, signed, witnessed.
  • 🟠 Notice must reach Postmaster/Manager CPC to be effective.
  • 🟡 Nomination can be cancelled/changed anytime before maturity.
  • 🔴 If nominee/legal heir is charged with murder, payment withheld until honourable acquittal.
Probable Q: In what 2 ways can a policy be assigned? Ans: Valuable consideration OR gift

💳 Manner of Realising Premium (Rules 43-50) 🔴 High-Yield

Premium due: 1st day of the month
Grace period till last day of calendar month
Pay-recovery policies: deducted from salary by month-end
Premia payable ONLY within India
Probable Q: Premium due date? Ans: 1st day of the month

🔧 Reduction, Discontinuance & Commutation (Rule 51) 🟡 Conceptual

  • 🔴 Paid-up option after 3+ years of premium payment.
  • 🟠 No bonus from discontinuance date; proportionate bonus after 5 years in force.
  • 🔴 NO alteration for AEA and Ten Year Rural PLI.

🌳 Surrender of Policies (Rule 55) 🔴 High-Yield

Surrender ફક્ત 3 વર્ષ (36 મહિના) પછી જ શક્ય.
Surrender केवल 3 वर्ष (36 महीने) के बाद ही संभव।
Surrender allowed after 3 years (36 months) — except AEA, 10-Year Rural PLI, Children Policy.
Probable Q: Minimum duration for policy surrender? Ans: 3 years

⚰️ Suicide Cases (Rule 61) 🔴 High-Yield

2 વર્ષની અંદર suicide થાય તો policy void ગણાય.
2 वर्ष के भीतर suicide होने पर policy void मानी जाती है।
Suicide within 2 years of acceptance/first premium (later) — policy VOID, no claim (except bona-fide third-party interest).
Probable Q: Suicide clause exclusion period? Ans: 2 years

⚖️ Murder by Nominee/Legal Heir (Rule 62) 🟡 Conceptual

Policyholder ની હત્યા nominee દ્વારા થાય તો, એ વ્યક્તિ કાયમ માટે claim receive કરવાથી debarred છે.
Policyholder की हत्या nominee द्वारा होने पर, वह व्यक्ति हमेशा के लिए वंचित है।
If policyholder is murdered by legal representative/nominee, permanently debarred from receiving policy money.
