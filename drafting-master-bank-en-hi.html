CASE 1 OF 5
Drafting a Demi-Official (D.O.) Letter to Expedite Pending Grievances
Scenario: Draft a D.O. letter from the Superintendent of Post Offices (SPOs) to an Executive Engineer, BSNL, requesting urgent settlement of pending telephone/internet breakdown complaints in Sub-Offices.
D.O. LetterInter-departmental
🇬🇧 English Version
BHARAT SARKAR / DEPARTMENT OF POSTS
Shri K.P. Sharma
Superintendent of Post Offices
Ahmedabad North Division
D.O. No: AN/BSNL-Maint/2026
Dated at Ahmedabad: 04.07.2026
Dear Shri Verma,
I am writing this letter to draw your personal attention towards the long-pending complaints regarding telephone and high-speed leased line connectivity breakdowns at our Kalol SO and Mansa SO under this division.
2. As you are aware, our post offices are completely dependent on online core banking and digital tracking platforms. Due to these frequent link failures, daily public counters are severely hit, leading to widespread public resentment. Our local staff has already logged formal complaints with your sub-divisional engineers, but no permanent solution has been deployed yet.
3. I shall be highly grateful if you could kindly intervene in this matter personally and direct your field maintenance teams to restore and stabilize the links on priority.
With warm regards,
Yours sincerely,

(K.P. Sharma)
To,
Shri R.K. Verma,
Executive Engineer (Telecom), BSNL,
Ahmedabad - 380001.

CASE 2 OF 5
Drafting an Office Memorandum (OM) calling for Explanations
Scenario: Draft an Office Memorandum from the Divisional Head to a Sub-Postmaster calling for an immediate explanation regarding a verified cash verification shortfall during a surprise visit.
Office MemorandumExplanation Call5-day deadline
🇬🇧 English Version
OFFICE OF THE SUPERINTENDENT OF POST OFFICES
AHMEDABAD NORTH DIVISION, AHMEDABAD
Memo No: AN/Inv/Cash-Short/2026      Dated at Ahmedabad, the 04.07.2026
OFFICE MEMORANDUM
During a surprise inspection conducted by the Assistant Superintendent of Post Offices (ASPOs) at Sabarmati SO on 02.07.2026, a physical cash deficit of ₹4,500/- (Rupees Four Thousand Five Hundred only) was detected in the office safe, which did not tally with the daily SO Balance Sheet.
2. Shri M.L. Gupta, Sub-Postmaster (SPM), Sabarmati SO is hereby directed to explain in writing why disciplinary action under CCS (CCA) Rules, 1965 should not be initiated against him for gross negligence, poor supervision, and non-compliance with the rules laid down in Postal Manual Volume VI.
3. The written explanation of the said official must reach this office within 5 days from the receipt of this memorandum, failing which it will be presumed that he has no explanation to offer and the case will be decided ex-parte.
(K.P. Sharma)
Superintendent of Post Offices
Ahmedabad North Division
To,
Shri M.L. Gupta,
Sub-Postmaster, Sabarmati SO,
Ahmedabad - 380005.

CASE 3 OF 5
Drafting an Official Letter to a Higher Authority (Postmaster General)
Scenario: Draft an official letter from a Divisional Superintendent to the Postmaster General (PMG) seeking financial and administrative approval for renting new premises for a Branch Office.
Official LetterFinancial Sanction
🇬🇧 English Version
DEPARTMENT OF POSTS / INDIA
From:
The Superintendent of Post Offices,
Ahmedabad North Division, Ahmedabad - 380002.
To:
The Postmaster General,
HQ Circle Region, Ahmedabad - 380009.
No: AN/Estt/BO-Rent/2026      Dated: 04.07.2026
Subject: Proposal for shifting of 'Chandkheda BO' to new rented premises and sanction of monthly rent – regarding.

Respected Sir/Madam,
I have the honor to submit a proposal for shifting the existing Chandkheda Branch Post Office (BO) to a new rented building due to the highly dilapidated and unsafe condition of the current premises.
2. The current building is leaking heavily during the monsoon, putting critical electronic DARPAN devices and small savings paper ledgers at high risk. A new central location measuring 250 sq. ft. has been identified, which is fully secure and structurally fit for hosting digital counter operations. The Fair Rent Certificate (FRC) has been obtained from the PWD authorities, assessing the monthly rent at ₹6,000/- per month.
3. In view of public safety and staff welfare, it is requested to kindly accord administrative approval and financial sanction for the proposed rent.
Encl: PWD Rent Certificate & Site Layout Blueprint.
Yours faithfully,

(K.P. Sharma)
Superintendent of Post Offices

CASE 5 OF 5
Drafting a Formal Office Order (Suspension under Rule 10)
Scenario: A Postal Assistant is caught red-handed in cash tampering and needs to be placed under suspension immediately. Draft an Office Order under Rule 10(1) of CCS (CCA) Rules, 1965.
Office OrderCCS (CCA) Rule 10(1)Suspension
🇬🇧 English Version
OFFICE OF THE SUPERINTENDENT OF POST OFFICES
AHMEDABAD NORTH DIVISION, AHMEDABAD
Order No: AN/Disciplinary/PA-Susp/2026      Dated at Ahmedabad: 04.07.2026
ORDER
Whereas a disciplinary proceeding against Shri R.S. Trivedi, Postal Assistant (PA), Ahmedabad HO is contemplated due to serious financial irregularities and manipulation of government cash accounts.
2. Now, therefore, the Superintendent of Post Offices, Ahmedabad North Division, in exercise of the powers conferred by Sub-rule (1) of Rule 10 of the CCS (CCA) Rules, 1965, hereby places the said Shri R.S. Trivedi under suspension with immediate effect.
3. It is further ordered that during the period this order remains in force, the headquarters of Shri R.S. Trivedi shall be fixed at Gandhinagar HO, and the official shall not leave the headquarters without obtaining the prior written permission of the competent authority. The official will be entitled to a subsistence allowance as per the rules.
(K.P. Sharma)
Superintendent of Post Offices
Ahmedabad North Division
Copy to:
1. Shri R.S. Trivedi, PA (Under Suspension).
2. The Postmaster, Ahmedabad HO / Gandhinagar HO for information.

आदेश सं: AN/Disciplinary/PA-Susp/2026      दिनांक अहमदाबाद: 04.07.2026
आदेश (Order)
चूंकि सरकारी नकद खातों में गंभीर वित्तीय अनियमितताओं और हेरफेर के कारण श्री आर.एस. त्रिवेदी, डाक सहायक (PA), अहमदाबाद एचओ के खिलाफ अनुशासनात्मक कार्रवाई करने का विचार किया गया है।
2. अतः, अब, अधीक्षक डाकघर, अहमदाबाद उत्तर मंडल, सीसीएस (सीसीए) नियम, 1965 के नियम 10 के उप-नियम (1) द्वारा प्रदत्त शक्तियों का प्रयोग करते हुए, उक्त श्री आर.एस. त्रिवेदी को तत्काल प्रभाव से निलंबित (Suspension) करते हैं।
3. यह भी आदेश दिया जाता है कि जब तक यह आदेश प्रभावी रहेगा, श्री आर.एस. त्रिवेदी का मुख्यालय गांधीनगर मुख्य डाकघर (HO) निर्धारित किया जाता है, और वह सक्षम प्राधिकारी की पूर्व लिखित अनुमति के बिना मुख्यालय नहीं छोड़ेंगे। कर्मचारी नियमानुसार निर्वाह भत्ते (Subsistence Allowance) के पात्र होंगे।
(के.पी. शर्मा)
