🧠 Quick Memory Trick

Assets = Liabilities + Capital — every single transaction, without exception, keeps both sides equal.

🔴 Expense / Loss / Drawings → always reduce Capital.

🟢 Income / Profit / Fresh Capital → always increase Capital.

🔵 A transaction can shuffle Assets internally (e.g. Cash → Bank) without changing the equation total.
