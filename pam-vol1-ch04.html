4.01 Introduction to Compilation 🔴 VI▾

PA Section maintains e-accounts DDO-wise, day/month-wise, and shares the verified abstract with e-Lekha via PFMS after current checks (per Chapter III). Day's account pushed on T+3 basis; monthly consolidation done by 8th of the following month.

Post-submission corrections are carried via Transfer Entries before consolidation, so source data GL-wise matches e-Lekha figures — preventing under/over-statement of revenue or charges.

PA Section verifies each day's DOP cash opening balance = previous day's closing balance, DDO-wise.

Non-computerised DDOs (not yet on CSI): accounts compiled per PAM Vol-I, 1st Edition 1986 provisions until brought onto Core Integration platform.

All TEs/reversals in PFMS require approval of AAO + Sr. Accounts Officer; details maintained manually DDO-wise & year-wise.

4.02 Book Section — Statements & Budget Control 🟠 FAQ▾

Book Section (HQ PA Wing) submits DoP's consolidated accounts to CGA via e-Lekha/PFMS, checking opening vs previous month's closing balance. It prepares:

(a) Quarterly Pay & Allowances Statement (b) Monthly Small Savings Statement (c) Monthly Agent Commission Statement (d) Review of Circle Non-Plan Expdr. Statement

Budget Control: PAO's Budget section continually monitors expenditure vs budget ceiling, DDO-wise, for scheme & non-scheme heads; escalates variations to HOD/HOC.

4.03 March Accounts Procedure 🔴 VI▾

Circle Accounts Office: On closure of March accounts, corrections for misclassification/errors/omissions made in March Supplementary / Journal Entry with Head of PAO's approval; corresponding TE posted in PFMS.

PA Wing of Directorate: March accounts closed as usual; no transaction left for supplementary that could be included in March itself. Book Section monitors SB/CC revenue, audit expenditure apportionment, IPPB revenue apportionment adjustments via TE.

Before submitting to CGA, a certificate is recorded confirming: (a) totals under each major head & Abstract pages checked (b) Abstract Appropriation Accounts (Central) figures tally with the Account.

Late adjustments after Supplementary closure → reported to Sr. DDG (PAF) with explanation from Head of Circle PAO. Second-batch Journal Entries must be passed by a Group A or B Officer, who flags avoidable delay to Director General.

Debt/Remittance between Circles: Unadjusted transfer advices by end-March → brought to DG's notice by debiting office. Debits to another Circle PAO after March require prior consent of the responding office.

🔴 Key date: Progressive March figures reported to CGA (MoF) by 15th June; Progressive Revenue/Service Charge statement to Budget Branch/PA Wing a week after account closure.

Annexure-I: Full Annual Accounts Closing Cycle (11 Steps) 🔴 VI▾

The complete year-end closing sequence, step by step:

1. Monthly Consolidation (PAO): CSI DDOs → daily accounts auto-shared, TE numbers auto-generated in PFMS. Non-CSI DDOs → PAO manually classifies into 15-digit e-Lekha heads, posts via manual TE (supervisor-approved). Consolidation due 8th of following month.
2. Consolidation at Book Section (Directorate): Views via e-Lekha (Controller Data Processing → Reports → Consolidated/Classified Abstract) & PFMS (CAM Reports → CMP02). After all 24 PAOs' accounts received, HQ overviews & submits to CGA; issues sent back to PAOs if problems found.
3. Monthly Submission at PAO: Digitally Signed Certificate (DSC) submission in PFMS at Pr.AO level, per Directorate deadline.
4. Monthly Submission at Directorate: All 24 PAOs consolidated at Pr.AO level ID → submitted to CCA level ID for approval → Pr.AO submits with DSC to CGA.
5. March Preliminary Accounts: Submitted like any monthly account (usual DSC process in e-Lekha & PFMS).
6. Review of Accounts: Book Section reviews monthly & quarterly; sends review file with PAF Wing objections (misclassifications, minus-plus bookings, no-budget-provision bookings) to PAOs for reconciliation via manual TE.
7. Time Schedule: CGA issues annual-accounts closing time schedule every March; binding on Directorate & all PAOs.
8. Supplementary-I Accounts: Opened in PFMS post March Preliminary, for left-out transactions + annual adjustments. Period-13 opened in CSI in parallel, closed per CGA schedule; PAOs then download, convert to 15-digit heads, post via manual TE.
9. Statement of Central Transactions (SCT): Generated by CGA in e-Lekha after Supplementary-I approval (no separate Supplementary-II in PFMS post-PFMS-introduction). DoP furnishes remarks on minus-bookings, marked variations, loan-head interest under MH0049. Submitted after Sr.DDG(PAF) approval.
10. Provisional Accounts: Book Section computes expected major-head corrections after SCT submission; prepared manually, signed by competent authority, sent to CGA.
11. JE Preparation & Supplementary-II (Final Accounts): JE Portal opens post-SCT approval; Periods 14/15 opened in CSI for correctness check; PAOs post JEs in e-Lekha across minor-head/DDO level → Directorate clubs & submits to CGA. Final PAO consolidation follows the usual monthly process.

🧠 "M-C-M-M-M-R-T-S-S-P-J" — Monthly(PAO)→Consolidate(Dte)→Monthly Submit(PAO)→Monthly Submit(Dte)→March Prelim→Review→Time schedule→Supp-I→SCT→Provisional→JE/Supp-II.
