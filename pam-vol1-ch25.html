Chapter XXV — Western Union International Money Transfer
अध्याय 25 — वेस्टर्न यूनियन अंतरराष्ट्रीय धन प्रेषण

▲
25.01 Introduction — Scheme Rules 🔴 VI▾

WUIMT (Money Transfer Service) = quick inward-only personal remittance from abroad to India beneficiaries (family maintenance, foreign tourists). Only inward transfers permitted.

RuleValue
Max cash payout per transaction₹50,000 (above this → A/c payee cheque or SB credit)
Foreign touristsHigher cash amounts permitted
Max transactions per beneficiary/year30 (RBI guideline)
DoP's commission share30% of WU's commission earned

WUFSI application is third-party, not integrated with DOP system — disbursements posted via direct posting. Payment posted against GL 878100103010000, credited to DOP cash / Drawn-from-Bank GL. Transactions posted transaction-ID wise, never in summary, to enable reconciliation.

25.02 Daily Settlement Flow 🔴 VI▾
WUIMT authorities daily exchange total payout + 30% commission + Spread Margin share + FX Gain/Loss + GST with SBI, Sansad Marg
↓
SBI Sansad Marg credits (net of GST) into Sr. Postmaster Sansad Marg HO's Deposit A/c
↓
SBI sends daily scrolls + date-wise reimbursement statement to FS Division, Directorate
↓
FS Division downloads Detailed & Consolidated Payout Reports via Voyager Module, checks settlement vs commission tally

Short credit of payout/commission → AO, FS Division claims from WUIMT authorities, including penal interest.

25.03 Role of FS Division (CSC) 🟠 FAQ▾

Classifies reimbursed amount: Principal received, Commission received, Spread Margin share, FX Gain/Loss. Verifies Sansad Marg HO's classification daily.

Circle-wise details to PAO Delhi by 5th of following month (Annexure-II format), reviewed by DDG(FS) on 6th
Monthly statement (CD) to Book Section, Directorate by 15th of following month
Claims for non-reimbursement/delayed-interest to DDG(FS) — weekly
Interest on delayed settlement = 12% per annum (Para 6.8, WU-DoP agreement), reviewed weekly
Security deposit review — quarterly (Para 6.4, WU-DoP agreement), based on avg. settlement reimbursement
Monthly Statement (CD) to all PAOs by 15th of following month for reconciliation

🔴 Key rate: Delayed settlement interest = 12% p.a., calculated from day after payment date till settlement.

25.04 Role of Head Post Offices 🟠 FAQ▾

DDO submits e-schedule (transaction-ID/voucher-wise, never consolidated) + vouchers to PAO by 5th of following month. WUIMT GL total must tally with total of all vouchers; invoice signed by designated staff.

25.05 Role of Postal Accounts Offices (14 Steps) 🔴 VI▾

PAO receives CD (Voyager Module) from FS Division → converts Circle data to HO/SO-wise → cross-verifies against e-Lekha → checks each voucher is in "To Receive Money" (TRM) format only.

🔴 Critical check: Any voucher NOT in TRM format will not appear in FS Division's Monthly Statement → results in loss to DoP. Non-TRM vouchers referred to Divisional Head immediately for correction via Western Union Money Gateway.

Reversed transactions by WU (causing loss to DoP) → immediately taken up with FS Division for recovery.

25.06 Role of PAO Delhi (Additional Duties) 🟠 FAQ▾

PAO Delhi ensures: WUIMT GL debit has a corresponding credit for the day; Sansad Marg HO (Nodal HO) correctly accounts all reimbursement heads (Principal, Commission, Spread Margin, FX Gain/Loss).
