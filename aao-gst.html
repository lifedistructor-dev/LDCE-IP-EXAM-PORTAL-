Definition परिभाषा

GST is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services, replacing multiple older indirect taxes with a single domestic tax system.

जीएसटी एक व्यापक, बहु-चरणीय, गंतव्य-आधारित अप्रत्यक्ष कर है जो वस्तुओं व सेवाओं की आपूर्ति पर लगाया जाता है और कई पुराने अप्रत्यक्ष करों को एक ही घरेलू कर प्रणाली से बदलता है।

📜 Tax System Before GST जीएसटी से पहले कर प्रणाली

▼
Important Concept 🟡
  • States collected via VAT (own rules per state)
  • Centre taxed inter-state sales via CST (Central Sales Tax)
  • Entertainment tax, octroi, local taxes → overlapping structures
  • Result: "tax on tax" / cascading effect (e.g., excise + VAT both on same goods) 🔴

⭐ Features of GST जीएसटी की विशेषताएं

▼
Unified/Single Tax Structure — combines multiple indirect taxes 🔴
Dual GST Model — CGST + SGST (intra-state), IGST (inter-state) 🔴
Destination-Based Taxation — tax revenue goes to consuming state 🟠
Input Tax Credit (ITC) — credit for tax paid on inputs, reduces burden
E-Way Bill System — digital waybill for goods movement
E-Invoicing — mandatory for certain businesses
Threshold Limits — small businesses below limit exempt
Composition Scheme — fixed % of turnover instead of regular GST
Online Registration & Compliance — registration, returns, payment online
Probable Q: Which GST is levied on inter-state supply? → IGST | On intra-state? → CGST + SGST 🔴

⚖️ Not Required vs Compulsory Registration छूट बनाम अनिवार्य पंजीकरण

▼

🔴 NOT Required — Sec 23

  • Exclusively supplying goods/services not liable to tax or wholly exempt
  • Category exempted by Govt notification (on Council's recommendation)
  • Agriculturist — to extent of supply of produce from cultivation

🔴 COMPULSORY — Sec 24

  • Inter-state taxable suppliers
  • Casual taxable persons
  • Persons paying tax under reverse charge
  • Non-resident taxable persons
  • TDS deductors (Sec 51)
  • TCS collectors (Sec 52)
  • Agents supplying on behalf of others
  • Input Service Distributors (ISD)
  • Suppliers via e-commerce operator
  • E-commerce operators themselves
  • OIDAR service suppliers (from outside India to India)

Sec 24 वाले व्यक्तियों को टर्नओवर सीमा चाहे जो भी हो, पंजीकरण करवाना ही होगा।

🧠 Mnemonic (Sec 24): "I-C-R-N-T-T-A-I-E-E-O" → Inter-state, Casual, Reverse charge, Non-resident, TDS, TCS, Agent, ISD, E-comm supplier, E-comm operator, OIDAR.
Probable Q: Is an agriculturist required to register under GST? → No (Sec 23, to extent of produce from own cultivation) 🔴

🗂️ GST Returns Overview (Sec 37-48) जीएसटी रिटर्न अवलोकन

▼
Important Sections 🔴

GST return provisions: Section 37 to Section 48, CGST Act 2017

Is aggregate turnover > ₹5 crore?
YES → Monthly filing required
NO → Can opt for QRMP (Quarterly Return, Monthly Payment) scheme
Probable Q: What is QRMP scheme threshold? → Turnover up to ₹5 crore 🔴

📤 GSTR-1 (Section 37) जीएसटीआर-1

▼

Return of outward supplies — filed by every registered person (except ISD, composition taxpayers, etc.)

Important Time Limits 🔴
Filer TypeDue Date
Monthly (turnover >₹5 Cr)11th of subsequent month (e.g., Aug → 11 Sept)
QRMP (turnover ≤₹5 Cr)13th of month following the quarter (e.g., Apr-Jun → 13 July)
🧠 Mnemonic: "11-Monthly, 13-Quarterly" — one-digit apart, easy to recall.
Details Furnished in GSTR-1
  • Invoice-wise details — B2B supplies
  • Consolidated details — B2C supplies
  • Debit/credit notes, exports, amendments
  • HSN code details
Rectification of Errors 🟠

Corrections via amendment in subsequent month's GSTR-1 — but NOT allowed after September of the following FY, or filing of annual return, whichever is earlier.

Important Penalties 🔴

Non-filing → late fee + penalty; may also block filing of subsequent returns like GSTR-3B (affects ITC flow).

Link with GSTR-2A/2B

Supplier's GSTR-1 data auto-populates into recipient's GSTR-2A and GSTR-2B, enabling ITC verification.

Probable Q: Last date to rectify GSTR-1 errors? → September of following FY or annual return filing, whichever earlier 🔴

📥 GSTR-2A (Section 38) जीएसटीआर-2ए

▼

GSTR-2A — auto-populated, read-only summary of inward supplies (purchases), generated from suppliers' GSTR-1 filings.

Supplier files GSTR-1
↓
Auto-populates recipient's GSTR-2A (purchases, imports, reverse-charge supplies)

Section 38 governs communication of details of inward supplies to the recipient.

Probable Q: Is GSTR-2A editable? → No — it is read-only/auto-populated 🔴

नोट: GSTR-2B भी इसी प्रकार का auto-generated statement है, जो एक निश्चित तिथि तक की आपूर्ति का सारांश देता है (static, ITC क्लेम हेतु प्रयुक्त)।
