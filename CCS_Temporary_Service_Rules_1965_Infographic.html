📘 Rule 1 — Title, Commencement & Application🟠
Short Title: CCS (Temporary Service) Rules, 1965 — supersedes 1949 Rules संक्षिप्त नाम: सीसीएस (अस्थायी सेवा) नियम, 1965 — 1949 नियमों का स्थान लेते हैं
Commencement: With effect at once (1st May 1965) प्रभाव: तत्काल प्रभाव से लागू
Made under: Proviso to Article 309 & clause (5) of Article 148 of the Constitution संविधान के अनुच्छेद 309 के परंतुक तथा अनुच्छेद 148(5) के अंतर्गत निर्मित

Q. How many categories of employees are excluded under Rule 1(4) of CCS (TS) Rules, 1965?
Ans: 7 (a–g)
📘 Rule 2 — Definitions🟡
"Appointing Authority" — authority declared as such under CCS (CCA) Rules, 1965. "नियुक्ति प्राधिकारी" — सीसीएस (सीसीए) नियम, 1965 के अंतर्गत घोषित प्राधिकारी
"Temporary Service" — service of a temporary Govt Servant in a temporary post, OR officiating service in a permanent post, under Govt of India. "अस्थायी सेवा" — अस्थायी पद पर अस्थायी सरकारी कर्मचारी की सेवा, या स्थायी पद पर स्थानापन्न सेवा
"Defence Service" — service under Ministry of Defence / Defence Accounts Dept., paid from Defence Services Estimates, NOT permanently subject to Air Force Act 1950 / Army Act 1950 / Navy Act 1957.
Note: Clauses (b) & (c) omitted by GSR 145, dated 22.2.1989.
🔑 Rule 5 — Termination of Temporary Service🔴 MOST IMPORTANT
Core Rule: Services of a temporary Govt Servant are liable to termination AT ANY TIME by notice in writing — given EITHER by the Govt Servant to the appointing authority OR by the appointing authority to the Govt Servant. अस्थायी सरकारी कर्मचारी की सेवा किसी भी समय लिखित सूचना द्वारा समाप्त की जा सकती है — कर्मचारी या नियुक्ति प्राधिकारी दोनों में से किसी द्वारा
Notice Period = 1 MONTH ⏱️ (Rule 5(1)(b))
Proviso — Pay in lieu of notice: Service may be terminated forthwith by paying a sum equal to pay + allowances for the notice period (or shortfall period).

🔽 Decision Tree: How Notice is Served (Note to R.5)

Appointing Authority wants to serve termination notice

Q. What is the notice period for termination of temporary service under Rule 5?
Ans: One month (either party can waive by pay-in-lieu).
Q. Within how many months can a termination case be re-opened under Rule 5(2)?
Ans: 3 months (exception: special circumstances recorded in writing).
📘 Rule 6 — Termination on Physical Unfitness🟠
Key Point: Services of a temporary Govt Servant may be terminated AT ANY TIME WITHOUT NOTICE if declared physically unfit for continuance by an authority competent to declare him permanently incapacitated (had his appointment been permanent). चिकित्सकीय रूप से अयोग्य घोषित होने पर बिना सूचना के किसी भी समय सेवा समाप्त की जा सकती है
Rules 3, 4, 7, 9, 11: Omitted / Deleted by GSR 145, dated 22.2.1989. Rule 8: Deleted.
Q. Under which Rule can a temporary Govt servant be terminated WITHOUT notice on medical grounds?
Ans: Rule 6.
💰 Rule 10 — Terminal Gratuity🔴 MOST IMPORTANT
Sub-rule (1): Temporary employee retiring on superannuation, discharged, or declared invalid — eligible for gratuity on SAME SCALE as permanent employees under CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972.
Sub-rule (1-A): Compulsorily retired as disciplinary measure → gratuity ≥ 2/3 of normal rate (but not exceeding full rate).
Sub-rule (1-B): Pension (instead of gratuity alone) applies if:
ConditionRequirement
Superannuation / permanently incapacitatedAfter 10+ years of temporary service ⏱️
Voluntary Retirement3 months' notice, after completing 20 years' service ⏱️
→ Eligible for superannuation/invalid/retiring pension + retirement gratuity; family gets family pension on death after retirement.
Sub-rule (2): Death in service → family eligible for family pension + death gratuity (same scale as permanent employees).

❌ No Gratuity Cases [Sub-rule (3)] 🔴

CaseDetail
(a)Resigns from post, OR removed/dismissed as disciplinary measure
(b)Re-employed after retirement on superannuation/retiring pension
Exception (1st Proviso): Resigned WITH PRIOR PERMISSION to join a Govt-owned/controlled corporation/company/body → paid terminal gratuity for Govt service rendered.
Exception (2nd Proviso): Absorbed in Central Autonomous Body (with parent dept. permission) → OPTION to count Govt service for pension under that body's scheme instead of drawing terminal gratuity.

📐 Calculation of Gratuity [Sub-rule (6)]

BasisRule
Pay for calculationPay drawn immediately before superannuation/discharge/invalidation/absorption/death + DA
'Pay' meaningAs defined in Fundamental Rule 9(21)(a)(i)
Extraordinary leaveCounted per Rule 21, CCS (Pension) Rules, 1972
Increment during ELIncrement earned during currency of EL (up to 120 days) counted for gratuity even if not drawn
Sub-rule (7): Rule 10 applies ONLY to Govt servants appointed ON OR BEFORE 31 December 2003 (i.e., Old Pension Scheme employees — NPS cut-off date). 🔴
Q. Minimum temporary service required for pension eligibility under Rule 10(1-B)?
Ans: 10 years.
Q. Rule 10 of CCS (TS) Rules applies to employees appointed up to which date?
Ans: 31st December, 2003.
Q. "Financed substantially" under Explanation to R.10 means what %?
Ans: More than 50% of expenditure met by cess/Central Govt grants.

📜 Important Case Laws🟡
Anil Kumar Das v. UOI (2011, Jhar): Colour-blindness detected only in 2nd medical exam after complaint, while similarly placed colour-blind constable retained — held discrimination, Article 14 violated.
Union of India v. Nand Kishor Aggarwal (2009, Del): Bona fide assessment of trainee's inability to cope with training justified termination; not punitive.
Union of India v. Anil Kumar (2019 SC): Termination following absence without leave application held simpliciter, not stigmatic.
Municipal Corporation of Delhi v. Prem Chand Gupta (2000, SC): Pay + allowances for notice period need NOT be paid simultaneously with termination order — must be paid within reasonable time.
Sudhakar Mishra v. UOI (2009, All): Suppression of pending court case in Verification Roll → valid ground for termination under Rule 5(1).
Narendra Kr. B. Bhandari v. Asstt. Director (1999, CAT): Notice pay is NOT mandatory to be mentioned with termination order under T.S. Rules.
K. Alex v. Delhi State Mineral Dev. Corp. (2009): TS Rules cannot be wrongly invoked for a regular (non-temporary) employee — termination held illegal.
Sujit Saha v. State of Tripura (2021, AIR Online Tri): Where termination cites stigmatic reasons (illegal money demand), a brief hearing opportunity must be given.
Gugan Singh v. DG, CRPF (2008, Del): Non-disclosure of criminal case involving moral turpitude in Verification Roll justifies termination.
Dr. N.G. Kulkarni v. UOI (2000, Del): A probationer remains probationer till confirmed; Rule 5(1) (applicable to temporary servants) cannot be invoked to terminate a probationer whose termination isn't simpliciter.

🧠 Memory Tricks / Mnemonics🟢
🖐️ "Rule 5 = Panja (Hand)" — 5 fingers, 1 month notice by EITHER hand (employee ya authority). Pay-in-lieu = "haath uthao, paisa do" (raise hand, forthwith pay & go). नियम 5 = पंजा — 1 महीने का नोटिस, कर्मचारी या प्राधिकारी दोनों तरफ से
🩺 "Rule 6 = Chhah mahine ka checkup nahi, seedha chhutti" — Physical unfitness = NO notice, straight termination.
🔟 "Rule 10 = Dus Saal, Das Gratuity" — 10 years temp service = pension eligible; "DAS" also reminds "Dismissed / Absconded(resigned) / Superannuation-reemployed" = No Gratuity (R.10(3): Resign, Removed/Dismissed, re-employed after pension).
📅 "31-12-2003 = OPS Deadline" — Remember "31/12/03" like a closing shop date — after this NPS applies, Rule 10 gratuity rule doesn't.
✉️ Notice Service Order: "PRG" — Person → Registered post → Gazette (in that priority sequence).

📝 One-Page Revision Sheet — CCS (TS) Rules, 1965

  • 🔴 Notified: 1st May 1965; supersedes 1949 Rules; under Art.309 proviso + Art.148(5)
  • 🔴 Applies to: civil post holders (no lien elsewhere) + work-charged staff (pension opted)
  • 🔴 7 exclusions incl. Railway servants, contract staff, non-departmental telegraphists (P&T)
  • 🔴 Rule 5: 1-month notice, either side; pay-in-lieu allowed forthwith
  • 🔴 Notice service order: Person → Regd. Post A.D. → Official Gazette (deemed served)
  • 🔴 Re-opening termination case: within 3 months (else special circumstances recorded)
  • 🔴 Rule 6: NO notice needed if physically unfit (competent authority declares)
  • 🔴 Rule 10(1): Gratuity same scale as permanent employees (CCS Pension Rules 1972)
  • 🔴 Rule 10(1-A): Compulsory retirement (disciplinary) → min 2/3rd gratuity
  • 🔴 Rule 10(1-B): 10 yrs service (superannuation/incapacitated) OR 20 yrs + 3-month VR notice → pension eligible
  • 🔴 Rule 10(3): No gratuity if resigned / removed / dismissed / re-employed after pension (exceptions: prior-permission resignation to PSU/body; absorption in autonomous body)
  • 🔴 Rule 10(7): Applies only to those appointed on/before 31.12.2003
  • 🟢 MCD v. Prem Chand Gupta: pay-in-lieu need not be simultaneous with termination order
  • 🟢 Forms exist for President-authority & other-authority notices/orders (4 standard proformas)
