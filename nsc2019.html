🔀 Flowchart — Type of Accounts 🔴 High-Yield

ફોર્મ-1 દ્વારા 3 પ્રકારના ખાતા ખોલી શકાય છે
फॉर्म-1 के माध्यम से 3 प्रकार के खाते खोले जा सकते हैं
3 types of accounts openable via Form-1
👤 Single Holder Type — adult self / guardian for minor / unsound-mind person / minor 10+ years
👥 Joint A-Type — up to 3 adults; payable to ALL jointly or to survivor(s)
👥 Joint B-Type — up to 3 adults; payable to ANY holder or to survivor(s)
Probable Q: Joint-B Type અને Joint-A Type વચ્ચે મુખ્ય તફાવત? Ans: Joint-B = payable to ANY holder; Joint-A = payable to ALL jointly/survivor

🌳 Decision Tree — Premature Closure 🟠 Frequently Asked

ખાતું maturity પહેલા ફક્ત 3 કિસ્સામાં જ બંધ થઈ શકે છે
खाता maturity से पहले केवल 3 मामलों में ही बंद हो सकता है
Account can close before maturity in ONLY 3 cases
(a) ખાતાધારકના મૃત્યુ પર (death of account holder)

(b) Gazetted Officer pledgee દ્વારા forfeiture

(c) Court order દ્વારા
1 વર્ષ પહેલા બંધ ➜ ✂️ ફક્ત મૂળ રકમ (principal only), કોઈ interest નહીં
1-3 વર્ષ વચ્ચે બંધ ➜ ✂️ POSA rate interest (complete months માટે)
3 વર્ષ પછી બંધ ➜ ✅ Table મુજબ ચોક્કસ amount (accrued interest સહિત)
🧠 Mnemonic: "DCP" યાદ રાખો — Death, Court order, Pledgee forfeiture — આ 3 જ કારણોસર premature closure શક્ય.
Probable Q: Premature closure ના 3 માન્ય કારણો કયા? Ans: Death, Court order, Pledgee (Gazetted Officer) forfeiture

🔒 Pledging of Account 🟠

ફોર્મ-3 દ્વારા pledge/transfer as security કરી શકાય છે, pledgee ના acceptance letter સાથે.
फॉर्म-3 के माध्यम से pledge/transfer as security किया जा सकता है, pledgee के acceptance letter के साथ।
Account may be pledged/transferred as security via Form-3, with pledgee's acceptance letter.
  • 🔴 President of India / Governor of a State (official capacity)
  • 🔴 RBI / Scheduled Bank / Cooperative Society (incl. Cooperative Bank)
  • 🟠 Public/private corporation or Government company
  • 🟠 Local authority
  • 🟡 Housing finance company approved by National Housing Bank
⚠️ Minor/unsound-mind person ના ખાતાનું transfer guardian ના written certificate વગર (કે વ્યક્તિ જીવિત છે અને transfer લાભદાયી છે) શક્ય નથી.
Probable Q: અંધ અથવા શારીરિક અસમર્થ વ્યક્તિ ખાતું કેવી રીતે pledge કરી શકે? Ans: કોઈ સાક્ષર વ્યક્તિને અધિકૃત કરીને (Para 6(6))

⚰️ Payment on Death — Nomination Rules 🟡 Conceptual

SituationRule
Nomination in forceNominee applies via Form-2 with death proof

🏛️ Important Authorities 🟡

  • Central Government — notifies scheme under Sec 3A of 1873 Act; power to relax hardship (Para 11)।
  • Accounts Office (Post Office) — opens/closes accounts, issues interest certificates, processes pledging/transfer.
  • Gazetted Officer (as pledgee) — may forfeit pledged account per scheme conformity.
  • Court — can order account closure/transfer.
  • National Housing Bank — approves housing finance companies eligible as transferees.

⚠️ Important Penalties / Deductions 🟠

SituationPayout
Closure before 1 yearPrincipal only — full interest forfeited
Closure 1-3 yearsPOSA rate interest for complete months
Closure after 3 yearsTable-specified amount (near-full accrued interest)

🚩 Important Exceptions 🟠

  • Premature closure allowed only in death / court order / pledgee-forfeiture — no voluntary premature closure otherwise.
  • Minor/unsound-mind person's account transfer needs guardian's written certificate of the person being alive and transfer being beneficial.
  • No maximum deposit or account-count limit — exception to most other small savings schemes.
  • Blind/physically-infirm persons can pledge via an authorised literate individual.
  • Central Govt. may relax any hardship-causing provision (Para 11).
