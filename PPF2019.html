🎯 Topic & Definition 🔴 Very Important

PPF એક 15-વર્ષીય, long-term, tax-saving (EEE status) બચત યોજના છે — Sec 3A, GSP Act 1873 હેઠળ notify.
PPF एक 15-वर्षीय, दीर्घकालिक, कर-बचत (EEE स्थिति) बचत योजना है — GSP अधिनियम 1873 की धारा 3A के तहत अधिसूचित।
PPF is a 15-year, long-term, tax-saving (EEE status) savings scheme, notified under Sec 3A of the Govt. Savings Promotion Act, 1873.
Short title: PPF Scheme 2019 Tenure: 15 years (extendable in 5-yr blocks) No Joint Account Governing Act: Govt. Savings Promotion Act 1873

🔀 Flowchart — Account Opening Limits 🔴 High-Yield

👤 Individual may open ONE account for self (Form-1)
🧒 One additional account per minor / unsound-mind person (as guardian)
⚠️ Only ONE guardian can open account for a given minor — not both parents separately
❌ NO Joint Account permitted under PPF
Probable Q: શું PPF માં joint account ખોલી શકાય? Ans: ના, PPF માં joint account permitted નથી (Para 3(3))

⚠️ Discontinuation & Revival 🟠 Frequently Asked

Minimum deposit ના ભરાય ➜ ખાતું "discontinued" ગણાય
Revival: ₹50 fee/year + arrears (₹500 × default years)
Un-revived discontinued account પર પણ scheme rate interest મળે છે
Discontinued account closed ના થાય ત્યાં સુધી નવું ખાતું ના ખોલી શકાય
Discontinued account માં loan/partial withdrawal facility ઉપલબ્ધ નથી
Probable Q: Discontinued account revive કરવા શું ફી છે? Ans: ₹50/year + arrears of ₹500 minimum deposit per default year

  • 🔴 Interest credited annually (at year-end).
  • 🟠 Interest based on lowest balance between 5th day and end of month — key exam trap!
  • 🟡 Interest credits regardless of account-office transfer during the year.
Probable Q: PPF interest ક્યા balance પર ગણાય? Ans: 5th day અને month-end વચ્ચેની lowest balance

🌳 Decision Tree — Loans (Para 8-9) 🟠

Eligible window: after 1 year, before 5 years from initial subscription year-end

Max loan: 25% of balance at end of 2nd year preceding loan year

Repay principal: within 36 months

After repayment: 1% interest (in ≤2 monthly instalments)

If not repaid in 36 months: 6% interest instead of 1% (from outstanding date)

Only ONE loan per year; no fresh loan till previous repaid fully
🧠 Mnemonic: "1-5-25-36-1-6" — Year 1 to 5 window, 25% max loan, 36 months repay, 1% normal interest, 6% penalty interest.
Probable Q: Loan સમયસર ના ભરાય તો interest rate શું થાય? Ans: 1% ને બદલે 6% p.a.

💸 Withdrawal (Para 10) 🟠

5 વર્ષ પૂરા થયા પછી, વર્ષમાં એક વાર withdrawal — 4th preceding year ના balance ના 50% સુધી (Form-2).
5 वर्ष पूरे होने के बाद, वर्ष में एक बार withdrawal — 4th preceding year की balance के 50% तक (Form-2)।
After 5 years, one withdrawal/year up to 50% of balance at end of 4th preceding year (Form-2).
  • 🔴 Withdrawal amount = lower of (50% of 4th-preceding-year balance) OR (50% of preceding-year balance)
  • 🟠 Outstanding loan + interest must be cleared before withdrawal
  • 🟡 Only from accounts NOT discontinued; only once per year
Probable Q: Withdrawal ક્યારે eligible બને છે? Ans: ખાતું ખોલ્યાના 5 વર્ષ પછી (year-end થી ગણતરી)

Probable Q: Extended block period માં મહત્તમ કેટલું withdraw કરી શકાય? Ans: Block ના start balance ના 60%

🌳 Decision Tree — Premature Closure (Para 13) 🔴 High-Yield

Form-5 દ્વારા, ફક્ત 3 કારણોસર, 5 વર્ષ પછી જ premature closure શક્ય
Form-5 के माध्यम से, केवल 3 कारणों से, 5 वर्ष बाद ही premature closure संभव
Premature closure via Form-5, only 3 grounds, only after 5 years
(a) જીવલેણ બીમારીની સારવાર — self/spouse/dependent children/parents

(b) ઉચ્ચ શિક્ષણ — self/dependent children (admission proof જરૂરી)

(c) Residency status માં ફેરફાર (Passport/visa/IT return proof)
⚠️ Premature closure પર interest 1% ઓછું મળે છે (scheme rate કરતાં) — ખાતું ખોલવાની/extension ની તારીખથી.
🧠 Mnemonic: "HER" — Health (life-threatening disease), Education (higher studies), Residency change — 3 premature closure grounds.
Probable Q: Premature closure પર કેટલો interest penalty છે? Ans: 1% ઓછો (lower by 1%) — Para 13(1)

⚰️ Closure on Death 🟡 Conceptual

મૃત્યુ પર ખાતું ફરજિયાત બંધ થાય — nominee/legal heir ચાલુ ના રાખી શકે (SCSS/NSC થી અલગ rule).
मृत्यु पर खाता अनिवार्य रूप से बंद होता है — nominee/legal heir जारी नहीं रख सकते (SCSS/NSC से अलग नियम)।
On death, account MUST be closed — nominee/legal heir CANNOT continue it (unlike SCSS/NSC).
Probable Q: મૃત્યુ પર PPF ખાતું nominee ચાલુ રાખી શકે? Ans: ના, ફરજિયાત બંધ થાય (Para 14) — SCSS/NSC થી વિપરીત

🛡️ Protection from Attachment 🟢 Quick Revision

PPF ખાતાની રકમ court decree/order દ્વારા debt/liability માટે attach ના કરી શકાય (Para 15).
PPF खाते की राशि कोर्ट के decree/order द्वारा debt/liability के लिए attach नहीं की जा सकती (Para 15)।
PPF account balance is protected from attachment under any court decree/order for any debt/liability (Para 15) — a unique legal shield.

🏛️ Important Authorities 🟡

  • Central Government — notifies scheme under Sec 3A of 1873 Act; power to relax hardship (Para 17); interest recoverable on defaulted loans accrues to Central Govt. (Para 9(4))।
  • Accounts Office (Post Office) — opens/closes accounts, credits interest, processes loans/withdrawals/extension।
  • Treating medical authority — confirms life-threatening disease for premature closure (ground a)।
  • Recognised institute of higher education — confirms admission for premature closure (ground b)।

🚩 Important Exceptions 🟠

  • PPF does NOT allow joint accounts — unique exception vs most other schemes.
  • On death, account MUST close — no continuation option (unlike SCSS/NSC where spouse/nominee can continue).
  • Once "continue without deposits" exceeds 1 year, cannot switch back to "with deposits" mode.
  • Discontinued account holder cannot open a new PPF account till the discontinued one closes.
  • Credit balance is protected from court attachment — unusual legal protection among small savings schemes.
  • Central Govt. may relax any hardship-causing provision (Para 17).
