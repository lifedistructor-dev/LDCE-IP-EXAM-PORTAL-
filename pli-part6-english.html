🏛️ Organization Roles & Responsibilities 🔴 High-Yield

Circle & Regional Office — Approvers for claims only
Division & Sub-Division — Appoint agents; Approvers for BOTH new business AND claims
Head Post Office (Counter) — Collect premium; initiate proposal
Head Post Office (CPC) — Full workflow, issuance, claim processing
Sub/Branch Post Office — Collection, indexing, RICT app usage
🧠 Mnemonic: "Circle=Claims-only, Division=Business+Claims" — Division has broader scope.
Probable Q: Which office approves BOTH new business AND claims? Ans: Division & Sub-Division Office
