👤 अध्याय 1 → CIF (Customer Information File) Commands
🔴 अति महत्वपूर्ण

CIF ग्राहक की मूल जानकारी संग्रहित करता है — हर नए ग्राहक हेतु सबसे पहला कदम।

1Duplicate CIF की जांचCDEDUP
2CIF CreationCCRC

5Verification के बाद ModificationCMRC
6CIF का SuspensionCMRC

📝 One-Page Revision Sheet

CIF: CDEDUP(duplicate check), CCRC(create/verify), CMRC(modify/suspend after verif.), HCCA(merger)
SB: CASBAO(open), HCAAC(close), HPBP(passbook), HALM(lien), HAFSM(freeze), HECSM(ECS)
Vault: HTCPIAE(balance view), HTM(cash move both directions)
Inventory: HISAI/HISIA(split own/other), HIMAI/HIMIA(merge), HICHB(issue chequebook)
TD/MIS: CMISAOP(open both), HTDREN(TD renewal), CLIMIT(MIS holding check)
SCSS/NSC: CSCAOP(SCSS open), CSCOAAC(NSC open), HDRP(NSC print)
RD (20 commands): CRDOAAC(open), HDSAMM(agent), LARDD(loan disburse), HLASPAY/HLAUPAY(scheduled/unscheduled repay)
EOB/BOD: HSCOD(change date), HSOLCOP(closure), HSCOLD(last day closure)
Outstation Cheque: HIRM(lodge+realize+dishonour सभी एक command)
PPF: CPDTM(deposit), CPWTM(withdrawal), Loan closure=CLPR→HACINT→HCAAC
Clearing: Outward=HOCTM, Inward=HICTMO/HRMI
Reports: HCRV(customer search), HACLI(ledger), HTCPIAE/HTCPIAC(teller position emp/currency)

⏰ Last-Day Revision Notes (सबसे ज्यादा Confusing Commands)

  • 🔢 CCRC vs CMRC = CIF Create/Verify(पहले) vs Modify After Verification/Suspend(बाद)
  • 🔢 CASBAOM vs CASBAM = SB Modification Before(O सहित) vs After(O रहित) Verification
  • 🔢 HACDET vs HACCDET = Account Details vs General Account Details (1 अक्षर अंतर)
  • 🔢 HISAI/HIMAI vs HISIA/HIMIA = Own Location vs Other Location (Split/Merge)
  • 🔢 HTCPIAE vs HTCPIAC = Teller Position Employee vs Currency
  • 🔢 HOCTM(Outward) vs HICTMO(Inward) = Clearing की दिशा पहचानें
  • 🔢 HLASPAY vs HLAUPAY = Scheduled vs Unscheduled Loan Repayment
  • 🔢 HCAACTD vs HCAAC = TD/MIS/SCSS Close vs SB/PPF Close
  • 🔢 PPF Loan 3-Step Closure = CLPR → HACINT → HCAAC (क्रम याद रखें)
  • 🔢 HIRM = Outstation Cheque के 3 काम (Lodge+Realize+Dishonour) एक ही Command से
