Chapter XV — General Provident Fund
अध्याय 15 — सामान्य भविष्य निधि

▲
15.01 Role of GPF Section & Governing Rules 🔴 VI▾

GPF Section handles: subscriber admission, correct subscription realisation, refund/withdrawal accounting, GPF GL correctness, individual account maintenance. Governed by GPF (Central Service) Rules 1960 + P&T FHB Vol-I.

🔴 Key eligibility rule: GPF subscription applies only to Govt servants who joined Central Govt service on or before 31.12.2003 (post-2004 joiners are under NPS instead).

15.02 Application & Nomination 🔴 VI▾

Application-cum-nomination examined per Rule 532, P&T FHB Vol-I; entered in Register of GPF Accounts — Form DG(PA)-77, serial number assigned (never re-allotted after account closure). Application filed in Guard File, destroyed after 3 complete account years.

Acknowledgement sent via Form GPF-3-D. Subsequent nominations use Forms GPF-9B/9C/9D/9E.

Nomination custody & verification: Kept under lock & key with Accounts Officer (Fund Section); verified annually by another AO in 3 batches over a 3-year cycle (~1/3rd nominations verified each year).

Cancellation notice (Rule 5(4), GPF Rules 1960) → nomination cancelled & returned immediately, even without a fresh nomination submitted.

15.03 Subscriptions & Schedule Checks 🟠 FAQ▾

Subscription checked against minimum/maximum rates; must match rate adopted in the first month of the year unless a permitted mid-year variation occurs. Over/short payment adjustable in subsequent months, but cash refusal is not permitted if subscriber requests recovery.

Schedule attached to every pay bill: Form TR-56 (A/c No., name, pay, subscription rate, amount, withdrawal refund).

🔴 Key date: GPF schedules & payment lists sent by Postal Accounts Section to Fund Section by the 12th of the following month.

Rate of subscription cannot be increased more than twice or reduced more than once in a year (checked at review stage).

15.04 Temporary Advances 🟠 FAQ▾

Checks: (I) sanctioned by competent authority (II) fund-rule conditions fulfilled. Sanction particulars noted in Ledger 'Remarks' column — advance amount, no. of instalments, amount per instalment, month of last recovery.

Non-receipt of any instalment credit tracked via the Register of Missing Credits.

15.05 Final Payment of Deposits 🔴 VI🔴

Departmental Officers must report death/retirement/dismissal of subscribers to GPF Section (per note to Rule 544, P&T FHB Vol-I) for prompt payment.

🔴 Key timeline: GPF final payment application disposed within 10 days of receipt (target, not automatic).

Pension Section sends Register of Pension Applications to Fund Section on the 7th of each month for retirement cases cross-check.

Interstate/Inter-circle payment: Authority (Form DG(PA)-78) sent by registered post, verified for Book No./Serial No., specimen AO signature, and special metallic embossing seal before countersignature by receiving Circle's AO(GPF). Disbursing Postmaster independently verifies signature + seal before payment.

Death before payment (no-family nominee case) → enquiry made re: possible subsequent marriage before authorising payment to nominee. Rule 33-A death benefit → separate payment authority with prescribed questionnaire (Annexure-III).

15.06 Calculation & Adjustment of Interest 🔴 VI▾

Annual interest per Rule 11, GPF Rules; interest updated in system, checked before accounting/issuing balance slips. Total interest credited to Fund, debited to "2049 – Interest Payments and other obligations."

Inter-department transfer: transferring dept debits 2049; receiving dept credits temporarily to "Interest Suspense" (S-Unfunded 8009), cleared to Fund Account at year-end (or before payment if account closes early).

Original interest calculation + Annual A/c prep + separate-clerk check → completed by 20th June
Accountant's detailed examination → by 28th June
AAO's detailed examination → by 15th August
Accounts Officer's detailed examination → by 15th September

🧠 "20-June, 28-June, 15-Aug, 15-Sep" — the GPF interest verification cascade (Calc→Accountant→AAO→AO).

15.07 Annual Account Statements & Ledger Review 🟠 FAQ▾

Post 31st March ledger closure, annual debit/credit statement sent to subscriber via DDO, acknowledgement of correctness obtained.

Ledger Review (Form Sy-3): Separate pages for (i) Schedules of deductions (ii) Ledger accounts (iii) Broadsheets. Monthly review of a portion of ledger; all ledgers reviewed at least once a year, tracked via Register of Selection.

Key review checks: opening balance = previous year's closing; pay on 31st March noted; subscription rate ≥ minimum; no more than 2 increases/1 decrease per year; non-recovery reasons noted; nomination receipt carried forward with attestation.

Accounts Officer reviews at time of: attesting declarations, authorising final payments, authorising Insurance Premium payments. Review register resubmitted within 3 days; submitted to Director/Dy.Director quarterly.

15.08 Objection Book & Unclaimed Deposits 🟡 Concept▾

Objection Book (Form GPF-19): Watches short/omitted subscriptions, unauthorised advance adjustments, missing sanctions/policy receipts, disbursement certificates. Objections communicated via Form GPF-22; adjusted via Adjustment Register — Form GPF-20.

Unclaimed Deposits (Rule 3(2), GPF Rules 1960): Transferred to "Deposits" at each year-end; separate register maintained, closed annually, tallied with ledger + Review of Balances. Refund only on Accounts Officer's authority.

Rule 33-B — Deposit-Linked Insurance: Applicable to deaths on/after 01.10.1991. Death after last working hour before midnight = treated as "death while in service."
