🪪 Accepted ID & Address Proof Documents 🔴 High-Yield

Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License, Post Office/Bank passbook — dual-purpose (ID+Address) documents chhe.
Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License, Post Office/Bank passbook — dual-purpose (ID+Address) documents हैं।
Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License, Post Office/Bank passbook — qualify for both ID and Address proof.
Proof of Identity
  • Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License
  • Voter ID, Ration Card (with photo)
  • CGHS/ECHS Card, PAN Card
  • MGNREGA card, Pensioner Card
  • Post Office/Bank passbook (with photo)
Proof of Address
  • Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License
  • Water/Telephone/Electricity Bill (≤3 months)
  • Income Tax Assessment Order
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate
  • Credit Card Statement (≤3 months)
🧠 Mnemonic: "3-Month Rule" — utility bills and credit card statements valid only if ≤3 months old.
Probable Q: How old can a Telephone Bill be as address proof? Ans: Not older than 3 months
