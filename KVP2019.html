🎯 Topic & Definition 🔴 Very Important

KVP એક "money-doubling" પ્રમાણપત્ર યોજના છે — જમા રકમ નિશ્ચિત મુદત પછી બમણી થાય છે, Sec 3A GSP Act 1873 હેઠળ notify.
KVP एक "money-doubling" प्रमाणपत्र योजना है — जमा राशि निश्चित अवधि के बाद दोगुनी होती है, GSP अधिनियम 1873 की धारा 3A के तहत अधिसूचित।
KVP is a "money-doubling" certificate scheme where the deposit doubles after a fixed maturity period, notified under Sec 3A of the Govt. Savings Promotion Act, 1873.
Short title: KVP Scheme 2019 Maturity (2020 onward accounts): 10 yrs 4 months No maximum deposit limit Governing Act: Govt. Savings Promotion Act 1873

खाता केवल 3 मामलों में maturity से पहले बंद हो सकता है
Premature closure allowed only in 3 cases (same as NSC)
(a) ખાતાધારકના મૃત્યુ પર

(b) Gazetted Officer pledgee દ્વારા forfeiture

(c) Court order દ્વારા

🏛️ Important Authorities 🟡

  • Central Government — notifies scheme under Sec 3A of 1873 Act; power to relax hardship (Para 11)।
  • Accounts Office (Post Office) — opens/closes accounts, processes pledging/transfer/maturity payment।
  • Gazetted Officer (as pledgee) — may forfeit pledged account।
  • Court — can order account closure/transfer।

🚩 Important Exceptions 🟠

  • Premature closure allowed only in death / court order / pledgee-forfeiture — same restriction as NSC.
  • Minor/unsound-mind person's account transfer needs guardian's written certificate.
  • No maximum deposit or account-count limit.
  • Blind/physically-infirm persons can pledge via authorised literate individual.
  • KVP does NOT qualify for Section 80C tax deduction (unlike NSC/SCSS/PPF) — key exam trap.
  • Central Govt. may relax any hardship-causing provision (Para 11).
