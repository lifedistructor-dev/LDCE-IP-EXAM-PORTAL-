1️⃣ Definitions (Rule 2) — परिभाषाएं

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

नियम 2 के अंतर्गत "Accounts Officer", "Emoluments", "Family", "Fund", "Leave" तथा "Year" जैसे मुख्य शब्दों को परिभाषित किया गया है।

Rule 2 defines key terms used throughout the GPF Rules including Accounts Officer, Emoluments, Family, Fund, Leave and Year.

📖 Important Rules / Key Terms

TermMeaning (Short)
Accounts OfficerOfficer assigned duty to maintain PF account (by Govt. or C&AG)
EmolumentsPay, leave salary, subsistence grant + dearness pay
Family (Male)Wife/wives, parents, children, minor brothers, unmarried sisters, deceased son's widow/children
Family (Female)Husband, parents, children, minor brothers, unmarried sisters (husband excludable by written notice)
ChildLegitimate + adopted child (as per personal law) + certain wards
FundThe General Provident Fund
YearFinancial year

⚠️ Important Exceptions

  • Wife judicially separated → no longer "family" unless subscriber intimates otherwise in writing.
  • Female subscriber can exclude husband from "family" by written notice.

🏛️ Important Authorities

Comptroller & Auditor General (for Audit cadre officers) / Government (for others) assigns Accounts Officer.

4️⃣ Nominations (Rule 5) — नामांकन

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

प्रत्येक अभिदाता (subscriber) को निधि में शामिल होते समय एक या अधिक व्यक्तियों को नामांकित करना होता है, जो उसकी मृत्यु पर राशि प्राप्त करेंगे।

Every subscriber must, upon joining the Fund, nominate one or more persons to receive the credit balance in the event of death.

📖 Important Rules

  • If subscriber has a family → nomination valid only in favour of family member(s).
  • Minor subscribers → nomination required only upon attaining majority.
  • Nomination can be cancelled anytime by written notice + fresh nomination.
  • Alternate nominee provision if primary nominee predeceases subscriber.

6️⃣ Conditions of Subscriptions (Rule 7) — अंशदान की शर्तें

🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

अभिदाता को निलंबन अवधि को छोड़कर प्रतिमाह अंशदान करना अनिवार्य है।

A subscriber must subscribe monthly except during suspension.

📖 Important Rules

  • Option to NOT subscribe during leave without pay or leave on ≤ half pay.
  • On reinstatement after suspension → may pay arrears lump sum or in instalments.
  • No subscription needed for month of quitting service (unless subscriber opts in writing beforehand).
  • After full withdrawal under Rule 32, no further subscription unless returns to duty.

⚠️ Exceptions

  • Group C/D staff of Survey of India on departmental leave — exempt.
  • Seasonal post holders — exempt during unemployment period.
  • No subscription during "dies non" period.
🧠 "Suspension में No Subscription, Return में Option"

8️⃣ Advances from the Fund (Rules 12–14) — निधि से अग्रिम

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

सक्षम प्राधिकारी अभिदाता को अधिकतम 3 महीने के वेतन या जमा राशि की आधी राशि, जो भी कम हो, तक अग्रिम स्वीकृत कर सकता है (Rule 12)।

Sanctioning authority may grant an advance not exceeding 3 months' pay OR half the credit balance, whichever is LESS.

📖 Purposes (Rule 12(1))

  • Illness/disability expenses
  • Higher education (India/abroad, beyond High School)
  • Betrothal/marriage/funeral expenses (customary)
  • Legal proceedings cost
  • Legal defence in departmental misconduct enquiry
  • Consumer durables (TV, washing machine, computer etc.)
  • Pilgrimage / places of religious eminence

  • Wrongful use of advance (Rule 14) → must explain in writing within 15 days; if unsatisfied, recovered in lump sum (or instalments if > half emoluments).
  • New advance normally not sanctioned until last instalment of previous advance repaid (except for special reasons in writing).
  • Advance under item (b) education — permitted once every 6 months (Note 3).

🔟 Conditions for Withdrawal (Rule 16) — निकासी की शर्तें

🔴 Very Important
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

एक बार में निकासी सामान्यतः जमा राशि की आधी या 6 महीने के वेतन से अधिक नहीं होगी, जो भी कम हो।

A single withdrawal normally shall not exceed one-half of the credit balance OR 6 months' pay, whichever is less.

TypeMax Limit
Normal½ of balance OR 6 months' pay (lower)
Special sanction — Clause (A) purposesUp to ¾ of balance
Clause (B) — House purposesUp to 90% of balance

⚠️ Important Rules

  • Subscriber must satisfy authority within reasonable time that money was used for stated purpose — else forced repayment.
  • House built/purchased with withdrawal money cannot be sold/mortgaged without President's permission.
  • Annual declaration required (by 31 December) on house/site possession status.

1️⃣3️⃣ Procedure on Death of a Subscriber (Rules 33, 33-A, 33-B) — अभिदाता की मृत्यु पर प्रक्रिया

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

Rule 33: यदि अभिदाता की मृत्यु राशि देय होने से पहले होती है, तो नामांकन/परिवार के अनुसार भुगतान होता है।
Rule 33-B (DLI Scheme): मृत्यु पर अतिरिक्त बीमा राशि (अधिकतम ₹60,000) — यदि न्यूनतम 5 वर्ष सेवा पूर्ण हो।

Rule 33: On death before payment, amount is paid per valid nomination, or to family in equal shares if no nomination.
Rule 33-B: Deposit-Linked Insurance additional amount (max ₹60,000) payable if subscriber completed 5 years service.

SituationPayment To
Valid nomination + has familyNominee(s) as per share specified
No nomination + has familyFamily members in equal shares
No family, valid nominationNominee(s)

⚠️ Important Exceptions (Rule 33)

  • No share to: major sons, major sons of deceased son, married daughters (husband alive), married daughters of deceased son (husband alive) — IF other eligible family members exist.
  • Widow(s) & children of deceased son together get the share that son would have received.

💰 DLI Scheme (Rule 33-B) — Conditions

  • Additional amount = average balance over preceding 3 years.
  • 🔴 Maximum additional amount: ₹60,000
  • Minimum 5 years of service required at time of death.
  • Minimum balance thresholds vary by Pay Band/Grade Pay (₹6,000 to ₹25,000).

1️⃣4️⃣ Manner of Payment of Amount in the Fund (Rule 34) — निधि में राशि के भुगतान की रीति

🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

जमा राशि का भुगतान केवल भारत में किया जाएगा; प्राप्तकर्ता को भारत में भुगतान लेने की व्यवस्था स्वयं करनी होगी।

Payments of GPF amount shall be made in India only. Recipients must make their own arrangements to receive payment in India.

📖 Procedure

  • Head of Office forwards subscriber's retirement/quitting details to Accounts Officer.
  • Accounts Officer issues payment authority at least 1 month before superannuation, payable ON date of superannuation.
  • First authority = first instalment; second authority issued after superannuation for subsequent contributions.

⚠️ Special Case — Lunatic Beneficiary

Payment made to appointed Manager (under Indian Lunacy Act 1912); if no Manager, via Magistrate/Collector's orders to person having charge.
