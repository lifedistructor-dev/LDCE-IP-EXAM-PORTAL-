General Conditions of Service 🔴 VERY IMP

सामान्य सेवा शर्तें — Fundamental Rules, Chapter III, FR 10 to FR 18

📖 Definition / परिभाषा 🔴
English

General Conditions of Service (Chapter III) lay down the basic rules governing a government servant's entry into service (medical fitness), the government's claim on his whole time, the concept of lien on a post, conditions for transfer, mandatory fund subscription, when pay begins/ends, treatment of unauthorized absence/strikes, and the ceiling on continuous leave.

हिंदी

सामान्य सेवा शर्तें (अध्याय III) किसी सरकारी कर्मचारी की सेवा में प्रवेश (मेडिकल फिटनेस), सरकार का उसके पूरे समय पर अधिकार, किसी पद पर "लियन" की अवधारणा, स्थानांतरण की शर्तें, फंड सदस्यता, वेतन प्रारंभ/समाप्ति, अनधिकृत अनुपस्थिति/हड़ताल तथा निरंतर अवकाश की अधिकतम सीमा को नियंत्रित करती हैं।

Probable Q: Under what conditions is a Government servant's lien on a post retained? List all five (FR 13 a–e).
⚠️ Important Exceptions 🟡
  • FR 10: Central Govt can dispense with medical certificate in individual cases or exempt a class by general order.
  • FR 15: Transfer to lower-pay post allowed if (a) inefficiency/misbehaviour, or (b) on written request, or (c) case covered by FR 49.
  • FR 17-A: Absence can be treated as NOT a break if competent authority (Appointing Authority) decides so.
  • FR 18: 5-year leave ceiling can be relaxed by President in exceptional circumstances.
  • FR 14-A: Lien terminates automatically on acquiring lien on another post outside the cadre (Central or State Govt.).
📅 Important Dates 🟢
  • FR came into force: 1 January 1922
  • Max continuous leave ceiling: 5 years (FR 18)
🏛️ Important Authorities 🟠
  • President — grants exceptions to FR 18 (leave >5 yrs), interprets rules (FR 8)
  • Central Government — makes rules on medical certificates (FR 10), fund rules (FR 16), determines competent authority for FR 17-A
  • Appointing/Competent Authority — decides break-in-service question under FR 17-A
⏱️ Important Time Limits 🟢
  • Continuous leave: not exceeding 5 years (default rule)
  • No fixed timeline for lien retention — governed by event (duty/leave/suspension continuing)
❌ Important Penalties 🔴
  • Unauthorized absence beyond authorized period → treated as break in service, affects LTC, quasi-permanency, and exam eligibility (FR 17-A).
  • Wilful absence after joining time expiry → may be treated as misbehaviour under FR 15 (cross-link to Topic 4).
🧠 Mnemonic (लियन कब बना रहता है): "DFJLS" — Duty, Foreign service/officiating, Joining time on transfer, Leave, Suspension. (FR 13 a-e)

Delegation of Powers under Fundamental Rule 6 🔴 VERY IMP

मूल नियम 6 के अंतर्गत शक्तियों का प्रत्यायोजन (Pay/Chapter IV area, Rule 6-8)

FR 6 — Central Govt delegates powers to officers
↓
EXCEPTION (a)
All rule-making powers
EXCEPTION (b)
Powers under Rules 6, 9(6)(b), 44, 45-A/B/C, 83, 108-A, 119, 121, 127(c)
↓
FR 7 — Delegated power exercised only after consulting Ministry of Finance
↓
FR 8 — Interpretation of Rules reserved to President only
📖 Definition / परिभाषा 🔴

English: FR 6 empowers the Central Government to delegate to any of its officers any power conferred on it by the Fundamental Rules, subject to conditions it may impose — but two categories of power can never be delegated: (a) the power to make rules, and (b) powers specifically listed under certain named rules.

हिंदी: मूल नियम 6 केंद्र सरकार को अपने किसी भी अधिकारी को मूल नियमों द्वारा प्रदत्त किसी भी शक्ति का प्रत्यायोजन करने का अधिकार देता है, परंतु (a) नियम बनाने की शक्ति और (b) कुछ विशेष रूप से सूचीबद्ध नियमों की शक्तियाँ कभी प्रत्यायोजित नहीं की जा सकतीं।

Probable Q: List the rules whose powers cannot be delegated under FR 6(b). / FR 6(b) के तहत कौन-सी शक्तियाँ प्रत्यायोजित नहीं की जा सकतीं?

🧠 Memory Trick: "6-9(6)(b)-44-45ABC-83-108A-119-121-127c" → Group as: Delegation(6) + Duty(9-6-b) + Allowance(44) + Housing(45ABC) + Leave(83) + JoiningTime(108A) + ForeignService(119,121) + Establishment(127c)
⚠️ Important Exceptions 🟡
  • FR 6 itself permits delegation "subject to any conditions" the Central Govt may impose — so delegation is conditional, not absolute.
  • FR 7 exception: Ministry of Finance may prescribe by general/special order cases where its consent is presumed to have been given (i.e., no fresh consultation needed each time).
🏛️ Important Authorities 🔴
  • Central Government — the delegating authority (FR 6)
  • Ministry of Finance — mandatory consultee for any delegated power exercise (FR 7)
  • President — sole authority to interpret the Fundamental Rules (FR 8)
⏱️ Time Limits & 🔴 Penalties 🟢

No specific time limit prescribed in FR 6-8; consultation with Ministry of Finance is mandatory before exercising delegated power (FR 7) — a procedural safeguard, not a numeric deadline.

Penalty angle: Any exercise of delegated power without required Finance consultation would be procedurally invalid/ultra vires.

🧠 Mnemonic: "Rule Making + 11 Rules = Never Delegated" — Remember it as 1 broad exception (a) + 11 specific rule-numbers (b).

Dismissal, Removal and Suspension 🔴 VERY IMP

बर्खास्तगी, निष्कासन एवं निलंबन — Chapter VIII, FR 52 to FR 55

FR 52 — Pay & allowances cease from date of dismissal/removal
↓
FR 53 — Subsistence Allowance during suspension
↓
FR 54/54-A/54-B — Reinstatement & pay fixation on appeal/review/Court order
↓
FR 55 — No leave during suspension
📖 Definition 🔴

English: This chapter governs the financial consequences of dismissal, removal and suspension — what a suspended employee is paid (subsistence allowance), and how pay/allowances are re-fixed if the employee is later reinstated by appeal, review, or court order.

हिंदी: यह अध्याय बर्खास्तगी, निष्कासन और निलंबन के वित्तीय परिणामों को नियंत्रित करता है — निलंबित कर्मचारी को क्या भुगतान मिलेगा (जीविका भत्ता), और बाद में अपील/समीक्षा/न्यायालय आदेश से पुनर्स्थापन पर वेतन कैसे तय होगा।

⚠️ Important Exceptions 🟡
  • No leave may be granted to a suspended Government servant (FR 55) — absolute rule, no exception listed.
  • FR 53(2) proviso: even without certificate, dismissed/removed/compulsorily-retired-but-deemed-suspended employees get the shortfall amount if they earned less than subsistence allowance elsewhere.
  • FR 54-B(2): if suspended employee dies before proceedings conclude, period from suspension to death = duty, full pay to family (adjusted against subsistence already paid).
🏛️ Important Authorities 🟠
  • Appointing Authority — orders suspension
  • Authority competent to order reinstatement — decides pay/allowance treatment on reinstatement
  • Court of Law — governs FR 54-A scenarios
⏱️ Important Time Limits 🔴
  • 3 months — trigger point for varying subsistence allowance rate
  • 50% — maximum increase/decrease in subsistence allowance after 3 months
  • 60 days — representation period before final pay-quantum order (FR 54, 54-A, 54-B)
  • 12 months — demand period for recovery of short-recovered licence fee (cross-reference, FR 45)
❌ Important Penalties 🔴
  • Dismissal/removal → all pay & allowances cease immediately (FR 52).
  • Delay in disciplinary proceedings attributable to employee → reduced pay on reinstatement (not full back-pay).
  • False certificate of non-employment during suspension → forfeiture adjustment against outside earnings.
🧠 Mnemonic: "3-50-60" → after 3 months allowance can change by 50%; reinstatement notice gives 60 days to reply.

CCS (Joining Time) Rules, 1979 🟠 FREQUENT

भारांतरण/नई पदस्थापना के समय जॉइनिंग टाइम नियम — FR Chapter XI (FR 105-108, 108-A) cross-refers to CCS (Joining Time) Rules 1979

Note: FR 105–107 are "Not Printed" in this compilation — the Fundamental Rules themselves direct readers to the CCS (Joining Time) Rules, 1979 for the detailed joining-time provisions. FR 108 and 108-A remain part of the Fundamental Rules and are exam-relevant.

FR 105-107 इस संकलन में मुद्रित नहीं हैं — विस्तृत नियमों हेतु CCS (जॉइनिंग टाइम) नियम, 1979 देखें। FR 108 व 108-A मूल नियमों में ही सम्मिलित रहते हैं।

📖 Definition 🔴

English: "Joining time" (FR 9(10)) means the time allowed to a Government servant to join a new post or to travel to/from a station to which he is posted. The CCS (Joining Time) Rules, 1979 govern its calculation, extension, and pay during this period.

हिंदी: "जॉइनिंग टाइम" (FR 9(10)) का अर्थ है वह समय जो किसी सरकारी कर्मचारी को नए पद पर कार्यभार ग्रहण करने या स्थानांतरण के दौरान यात्रा हेतु दिया जाता है।

⚠️ Important Exceptions 🟡
  • FR 108-A special category: private-sector appointee gets joining time treatment both ways (joining & reversion), with pay capped at the lower of two comparators.
  • Joining time granted under FR 105(a)/(c) counts for increment differently than under FR 105(b) — depends on which post's time-scale applies.
🏛️ Important Authorities 🟠
  • Competent/Appointing Authority — sanctions and can extend joining time under CCS (JT) Rules 1979
  • Government (via 108-A) — decides discretionary treatment for private-employment appointees
❌ Important Penalties 🔴
  • No pay or leave salary after joining time expires without joining (FR 108).
  • Wilful absence beyond joining time → may be treated as misbehaviour, inviting action under FR 15 (transfer/disciplinary consequences).
🧠 Mnemonic: "No Join → No Pay, Wilful → Naughty (Misbehaviour)" — remember FR 108's two-part consequence.

Very Imp Frequent Concept Quick

1️⃣ General Conditions of Service

  • 🔴 FR 10 = Medical cert before appointment
  • 🔴 FR 13 = Lien retained (DFJLS): Duty, Foreign/officiating, Joining time, Leave, Suspension
  • 🟠 FR 15 = No transfer to lower pay except inefficiency/misbehaviour/written request
  • 🟢 FR 18 = Max continuous leave = 5 years

2️⃣ FR 6 Delegation

  • 🔴 Non-delegable: (a) all rule-making + (b) Rules 6, 9(6)(b), 44, 45-A/B/C, 83, 108-A, 119, 121, 127(c)
  • 🟠 FR 7 = Ministry of Finance consultation mandatory (consent can be "presumed" by general order)
  • 🟡 FR 8 = Only President interprets FRs

3️⃣ Dismissal, Removal & Suspension

  • 🔴 FR 52 = Pay ceases from dismissal/removal date
  • 🔴 FR 53 = Subsistence = half pay/half avg pay + DA; ±50% variable after 3 months
  • 🔴 FR 54 = 60 days representation before reduced-pay order on reinstatement
  • 🟢 FR 55 = No leave during suspension

4️⃣ CCS (Joining Time) Rules 1979

  • 🔴 FR 9(10) = Joining time definition
  • 🔴 FR 108 = No join in time → no pay/leave salary; wilful = misbehaviour under FR 15
  • 🟡 FR 108-A = Private employment → Govt: pay = lower of private/Govt. pay during transit
