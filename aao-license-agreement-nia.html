Definition परिभाषा

Telecom License Agreements are legal contracts issued by the regulator (DoT) granting operators permission to provide services within defined financial & operational frameworks.

टेलीकॉम लाइसेंस एग्रीमेंट वे कानूनी अनुबंध हैं जो नियामक (DoT) द्वारा ऑपरेटरों को निर्धारित वित्तीय व परिचालन ढांचे के भीतर सेवाएं प्रदान करने की अनुमति देते हैं।

Important Authority महत्वपूर्ण प्राधिकरण
  • Licensor: Department of Telecommunications (DoT) 🔴
  • Regulator: TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India) 🟠
  • Space Segment Authority: Department of Space (DoS) / NOCC
Probable Q: Who is the "Licensor" under Unified License? "लाइसेंसर" कौन है? → DoT

💰 Financial Conditions वित्तीय शर्तें

▼
Micro Topic: Entry Fee 🔴
Entry Fee — One-time, non-refundable, per authorized service
Max: ₹15 Crore
Micro Topic: License Fee (LF) 🔴
Annual LF = % of AGR, paid service-area-wise
↓
LF = 8% of AGR (includes USO Levy, presently 5% of AGR)
↓
From 2nd year: LF ≥ 10% of Entry Fee (minimum floor)
Probable Q: LF is what % of AGR? LF, AGR का कितना % है? → 8% (USO levy 5% included)
Nano Topic: Space Segment Charges 🟡
  • Payable to Department of Space (DoS) for satellite-based service
  • Monitoring charges payable to NOCC (Network Operations Control Centre)
Micro Topic: Payment Schedule 🟠
Quarterly
LF payable in 4 quarterly instalments, FY starts 1st April
Within 15 days
Adjustment of last quarter's advance vs. actual dues
On delay
Interest = 2% above 1-yr MCLR of SBI (as on 1st April), compounded Annually

विलंब पर ब्याज = SBI की 1-वर्षीय MCLR + 2%, वार्षिक रूप से चक्रवृद्धि

Probable Q: Interest rate on delayed LF payment? → SBI 1-yr MCLR + 2%, compounded annually 🔴

🏦 Bank Guarantees बैंक गारंटी

▼

PBG 🔴

Performance Bank Guarantee
Max: ₹44 Cr (prev ₹220 Cr)
Validity: 1 year
Purpose: covers violation of license conditions

FBG 🔴

Financial Bank Guarantee
Max: ₹8.8 Cr (prev ₹44 Cr)
= 20% of estimated LF for 2 quarters + other dues
Reviewed every 6 months
Important Rules महत्वपूर्ण नियम
  • BGs must be renewed/extended at least 1 month before expiry by Licensee on its own
  • Failure to renew → violation → Licensor can encash & convert to cash security
  • No interest/compensation payable by Licensor on encashment
  • Partially encashed BG must be restored to full amount
🧠 Mnemonic: "PBG Performs, FBG Finances" — P for Performance (violation cover), F for Financial (dues cover)
याद रखें: PBG = परफॉर्मेंस (उल्लंघन कवर), FBG = फाइनेंशियल (बकाया कवर)
Probable Q: Current max PBG amount? → ₹44 Crore | Current max FBG amount? → ₹8.8 Crore

📊 GR → ApGR → AGR सकल राजस्व से समायोजित सकल राजस्व

▼
Gross Revenue (GR)
All income — service, leasing, fees, calls, equipment sale, interest, dividends. No set-off of expenses.
↓ minus
= Applicable Gross Revenue (ApGR)
Excludes: (i) non-telecom revenue (ii) I&B Ministry license revenue (iii) USO Fund receipts (iv) dividend, interest, capital gains, forex gains, property rent, insurance claims, bad debts recovered, excess provisions written back
↓ minus
= Adjusted Gross Revenue (AGR)
Excludes from ApGR: (a) pass-through charges to other telecom operators (b) GST paid to Govt
🧠 Mnemonic for ApGR exclusions: "DIC-GIBE" → Dividend, Interest, Capital gains, Gains(forex), Insurance, Bad debts recovered, Excess provisions
ApGR से घटाई जाने वाली मदें याद रखने की ट्रिक
Probable Q: What is excluded from ApGR to get AGR? → Pass-through charges + GST 🔴

🕒 NIA Timeline (2010–2024) एनआईए समयरेखा

▼

NIA = Notice Inviting Applications — स्पेक्ट्रम नीलामी हेतु आधिकारिक दस्तावेज़ जो DoT द्वारा जारी होता है।

NIA 2010 🔴
First 3G + BWA auction. Bands: 2100 MHz (3G), 2300 MHz (BWA). SUC = % of AGR.
NIA 2012
Post Supreme Court cancellation of 122 2G licenses. Band: 1800 MHz. SUC: 3%–8% of AGR.
NIA 2013
Re-auction for 2012 losers. Bands: 1800, 900 MHz (new). SUC: weighted average.
NIA 2014
Renewal/reallocation. Bands: 900, 1800 MHz. SUC: weighted average.
NIA 2015
Major 4G auction. Bands: 800, 900, 1800, 2100 MHz. SUC: weighted average.
NIA 2016
Large-scale 4G. Band 700 MHz introduced (unsold). Also 800/900/1800/2100/2300/2500 MHz.
NIA 2021 🟠
Post-COVID 4G+. Zero SUC for new spectrum reform begins. Legacy holdings: weighted average SUC.
NIA 2022 🔴
First 5G auction. Bands: 600, 700 MHz, 3.3-3.67 GHz (mid), 26 GHz (mmWave). Zero SUC for new spectrum.
NIA 2023
2nd 5G round. Same bands as 2022. Zero SUC continues for new acquisitions.
NIA 2024
Continuation of 5G expansion, more spectrum availability.
🧠 Mnemonic: "2021 se Zero" — Yaad rakho: 2021 se SUC reform shuru, naye spectrum par SUC ZERO hai (sirf legacy holdings par weighted average lagta hai).
Probable Q: From which NIA year is SUC zero for new spectrum? → NIA 2021 🔴

🧮 Numerical Formulas & Solved Examples सूत्र व हल किए गए उदाहरण

▼
Core Formulas 🔴
AGR = Service Revenue + Other Income − Non-Telecom Revenue
SUC = SUC Rate % × AGR
Minimum Annual LF = 10% × Entry Fee (from 2nd year)
Minimum Quarterly LF = Annual Minimum LF ÷ 4
Pro-rata LF = (Days in effect ÷ Days in full quarter) × Full Quarter LF
Example: AGR & SUC
ItemValue
Service Revenue₹500 Cr
Other Income₹50 Cr
Non-Telecom Revenue₹10 Cr (excluded)
AGR500+50−10 = ₹540 Cr
SUC @5%5% × 540 = ₹27 Cr
Rule: Minimum LF Floor 🟠

If actual LF (8% of AGR) is less than the minimum floor (10% of Entry Fee ÷ 4 per quarter), the higher (minimum floor) amount is payable — especially critical for the first quarter.

यदि वास्तविक LF, न्यूनतम फ्लोर से कम है, तो न्यूनतम राशि देय होगी (विशेषकर पहली तिमाही में)।

Rule: Pro-rata for Mid-Year Start 🟠

If license effective mid-quarter, LF = (days effective ÷ total days in quarter) × full quarter LF.

Example: License from 15.11 → 47 days/92 days × ₹12,000 = ₹6,130
Rule: Excess LF Paid 🟢

Any excess license fee paid gets adjusted in the next financial year — not refunded.

Rule: Q4 Floor 🟡

License fee payable for the 4th quarter should not be less than the license fee paid during the 3rd quarter.

Probable Q (numerical): Entry Fee ₹2,00,000. What is minimum quarterly LF? → 10% of 2,00,000 = ₹20,000/year → ₹5,000/quarter 🔴

🧠 Memory Tricks & Mnemonics याद रखने की तरकीबें

▼
"8-5-10" → LF is 8% of AGR (5% is USO within it), minimum floor is 10% of Entry Fee.
"PBG Punishes, FBG Funds" → PBG covers violations/performance; FBG covers financial dues.
"44-8.8" → PBG max ₹44 Cr is exactly 5× FBG max ₹8.8 Cr — easy to cross-check in exam.
"3-3-1" → Records preserved 3 years, Auditor report in 3 months, BG renewal 1 month before expiry.
"2021 Zero, 2022 Panch-G" → 2021 = Zero SUC begins; 2022 = 5G ("Panch-G") auction begins.
