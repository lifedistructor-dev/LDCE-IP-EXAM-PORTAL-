2️⃣ Handling Loan Cases 🔴 High-Yield

Loan facility badhi PLI/RPLI policy par — AEA, 10-Year RPLI, Children Policy sivay.
Loan facility सभी PLI/RPLI पॉलिसी पर — AEA, 10-Year RPLI, Children Policy को छोड़कर।
Loan facility on all PLI/RPLI policies EXCEPT AEA, 10-Year RPLI, Children's Policy.
Loan from India Post ➜ Loan Application (Annex-II)

Loan from other organisation ➜ Assignment Letter (Annex-III)
Missing document window Loan mate 7 days j chhe (Maturity/Death claim ma 15 days).
Missing document window Loan के लिए केवल 7 दिन है (Maturity/Death claim में 15 दिन)।
Missing document window for Loan is only 7 days (vs 15 days for Maturity/Death claims).
Probable Q: Which 3 policy types are NOT eligible for loan? Ans: AEA, 10-Year RPLI, Children's Policy

3️⃣ Handling Death Claim Cases 🔴 Very High-Yield

Citizen Charter: 30 days normal, 90 days Inquiry required.
Citizen Charter: 30 दिन सामान्य, 90 दिन Inquiry required।
Citizen Charter: 30 days normal claims, 90 days claims requiring Inquiry.
Death after 3 years from acceptance/revival ➜ NO Inquiry

Death within 3 years ➜ Inquiry required
🧠 Mnemonic: "3-Year Rule" — insurer can question policy for misrepresentation only within 3 years. After that, no inquiry needed.
