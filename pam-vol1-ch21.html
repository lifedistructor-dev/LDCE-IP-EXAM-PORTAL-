21.01 Introduction — Business Development 🔴 VI▾

Business Development Directorate = DoP's commercial arm — formulates/operationalises value-added services (Speed Post, Business Post, Business Parcel etc.) and monitors them.

Separate sub-heads & detailed heads opened to classify revenue/expenditure per product, enabling economic viability analysis. Monitoring split by walk-in customer, registered customer, and registered contract customer — with the overall goal of no under/over-statement of revenue.

21.02 Product Categories under Business Development 🟡 Concept▾

Broad product families (each with its own PRC — Postage Realised in Cash — revenue line):

Speed Post (Document/Parcel/COD/E-commerce) Business Post / Business Parcel Greeting Post / Express Parcel Post Direct Post / Mass Mailing Services Bill Mail Service / National Bill Mail / E-Post Philately (stamps, My Stamps) Flat-rate Parcel / Print-to-Post / e-VPP International EMS/Parcel/Registered Letters/e-Packet Retail Post (Gangajal, tickets, forms, SIM/recharge) e-Billers / Media Post / Logistics Post Financial products (SBI cards, mutual funds, WUFSI, IPPB penal interest, PMJJBY, PMSBY, APY handling charge)

🧠 For exam purposes, know the categories (Speed Post, Business Post, Retail Post, e-Commerce, Financial Products) rather than every individual line item — the manual lists 60+ granular revenue heads.

21.03 Accrual vs Cash — Core Principle 🔴 VI▾

SOPs issued by Business Development / Postal Operations / Parcel Directorate / PBI Division govern each service. Products offered on credit (BNPL — Buy Now Pay Later) as well as with advances.

🔴 Core rule: Since CGA submission is cash-based accounting, the CGA report must never contain accruals (receivables/payables) unless actually received/paid. Accruals are only e-data for monitoring — accountant must ensure accruals never leak into the CGA report.

21.04 The 16-Point Check List (a–p) 🔴 VI▾
aGL-wise e-schedule tallies with DDO-CGA report daily & on monthly PFMS consolidation
bErrors/misclassification rectified via Transfer Entry against the DDO
cBNPL customer dues + penal interest + statutory taxes fully realised
dAdvance-payment customer accounts adjusted/settled against invoices per periodicity
eDues paid direct to Postmaster's bank account accounted immediately on receipt scroll
fCOD/e-Biller credits checked against payments; erroneous payments flagged to DDO immediately
gRegistered customer accounts checked for duplicate payments
hCustomer account matches business rules + signed agreement (kept in guard file)
iStatutory taxes correctly applied per GST rules
jDiscount/rebate/commission/incentive rates correctly applied
kDual BNPL+COD customers: invoice issue & dues-received accounting cross-checked
lObjection registers (other than HR) maintained, followed up with DDO/HOD
mOnly net amount payable to customer released (fee/commission retained correctly)
nAll DDO settlements against receivables reflected in CGA report
oOpen items reported electronically to DDOs for realisation
pNo direct manual posting against customer settlement — always via system workflow

🧠 Group the 16 checks into 4 themes: Reconciliation (a,b,n), Customer Dues (c,d,e,f,g,k), Compliance (h,i,j), Process Control (l,m,o,p).
