🎯 Topic & Definition 🔴 Very Important

Post Office Insurance Fund (Custody and Investment) Regulations, 2016 — 1 November 2009 થી deemed effective, POLI Rules 2011 ના Rule 3 હેઠળ.
Post Office Insurance Fund (Custody and Investment) Regulations, 2016 — 1 नवंबर 2009 से deemed effective, POLI Rules 2011 के Rule 3 के तहत।
POIF Regulations 2016 — deemed in force from 1 November 2009, made under Rule 3 of POLI Rules 2011. Governs custody, investment, receipts, and disbursements of the Fund.

📖 Key Definitions 🟠

  • The Fund: Post Office Insurance Fund — PLI & RPLI collections in Public Account of India.
  • Sub Funds: PLIF (incl. Civil & Military PLI) and RPLIF.
  • Net Corpus: Total premiums as of close of 31 October 2009.
  • Net Accretions: Deposits − payments from 1 November 2009 onward.
  • Custodian: SEBI-registered institution holding PLIF/RPLIF custodian accounts.
  • CIO: Heads Investment Division; HAG-rank officer.
Probable Q: What are the two sub-funds? Ans: PLIF and RPLIF

🏛️ Governance Structure 🔴 High-Yield

Postal Services Board — highest decision body
Investment Board — Chairman: Member (PLI), Postal Services Board
Investment Division — headed by CIO (HAG rank)
Fund/Portfolio Manager(s) — 2-year contract
Custodian Bank — holds securities/investments
Investment Board ના Chairman: Member (PLI), Postal Services Board.
Investment Board के Chairman: Member (PLI), Postal Services Board।
Investment Board composition: Chairman = Member (PLI); + 3 external Financial Experts, JS (Finance/DoP), CGM (PLI), CIO, Actuary.
Probable Q: Who chairs the Investment Board? Ans: Member (PLI), Postal Services Board

💰 Receipts vs Disbursements 🟠

✅ Receipts:
  • Premium/subscription deposits, loan repayments+interest, investment discharge amounts, interest/dividends, bonus shares
📤 Disbursements:
  • Loans, maturity/death claims, surrender values, investments, bonus payable, admin expenses

📈 Investment Rules 🟡 Conceptual

Net Corpus (31 Oct 2009 સુધીનો) freeze કરી, 3 વર્ષમાં dated securities માં convert થાય. નવી Net Accretions IRDA norms હેઠળ invest થાય.
Net Corpus (31 अक्टूबर 2009 तक का) freeze कर, 3 वर्ष में dated securities में convert होता है। नई Net Accretions IRDA norms के तहत invest होती हैं।
Net Corpus frozen & converted into dated securities over 3 years (from FY 2010-11). Net Accretions invested per IRDA (Investment) Regulations, 2000.
🧠 Mnemonic: "Freeze the Old, Invest the New" — pre-2009 corpus frozen/converted; post-2009 accretions actively invested.
Probable Q: Over how many years is frozen Net Corpus converted? Ans: 3 years
