🎯 Topic & Definition 🔴 Very Important

SSY એ ગર્લ ચાઈલ્ડ (10 વર્ષથી નાની) માટેની ખાસ બચત યોજના છે — 21 વર્ષે maturity, ઉચ્ચ વ્યાજ દર, Sec 3A GSP Act 1873 હેઠળ notify.
SSY एक बालिका (10 वर्ष से कम आयु) के लिए विशेष बचत योजना है — 21 वर्ष पर maturity, उच्च ब्याज दर, GSP अधिनियम 1873 की धारा 3A के तहत अधिसूचित।
SSY is a special savings scheme for a girl child (under 10 years), maturing at 21 years, offering a high interest rate, notified under Sec 3A of the Govt. Savings Promotion Act, 1873.
Short title: SSY Scheme 2019 Maturity: 21 years from opening Deposits allowed: 15 years only Governing Act: Govt. Savings Promotion Act 1873

🔀 Flowchart — Eligibility & Account Opening 🔴 High-Yield

Girl child ની ઉંમર ખાતું ખોલવાના સમયે 10 વર્ષથી ઓછી હોવી જોઈએ.
बालिका की आयु खाता खोलने के समय 10 वर्ष से कम होनी चाहिए।
Girl child must be under 10 years of age on the date of opening.
👧 Girl child, age < 10 years, opened by guardian (Form-1 + birth certificate)
📋 One girl child = ONE account only
👨‍👩‍👧‍👧 Maximum 2 accounts per family (general rule)
👯 Exception: twins/triplets in 1st or 2nd birth order → more than 2 accounts, with affidavit + birth certificates
⚠️ Exception does NOT apply if 1st birth order already resulted in 2+ surviving girls
Probable Q: સામાન્ય રીતે એક family માં કેટલા SSY ખાતા ખોલી શકાય? Ans: મહત્તમ 2 (twins/triplets exception સિવાય)

⚠️ Default & Regularisation 🟠 Frequently Asked

Minimum ₹250/year ના ભરાય ➜ ખાતું "under default" ગણાય
Regularise: ₹50 penalty/year + minimum deposit for defaulted years
Regularise ની સમય-મર્યાદા: 15 વર્ષ પૂરા થાય ત્યાં સુધી
Regularise ના થાય તો ➜ આખી deposit પર scheme rate interest, closure સુધી (કોઈ penalty ના, પણ default clear ના થાય)
Probable Q: Default account regularise કરવાની ફી શું છે? Ans: ₹50/year + minimum deposit arrears

📈 Interest on Deposit 🔴

PeriodRate

Current Rate — Apr–Jun 20268.2% p.a. (among highest of all small savings schemes)
✅ SSY currently offers one of the highest interest rates among Post Office schemes — reflecting its policy priority under Beti Bachao Beti Padhao.
  • 🔴 Interest calculated on lowest balance between 5th day and month-end (same rule as PPF).
  • 🟠 Interest credited at end of financial year (not calendar year).
  • 🟡 Fraction rounding: ≥50 paisa rounds up, <50 paisa ignored.
  • 🟢 Interest credits regardless of account-office transfer during the year.
Probable Q: SSY interest ક્યા balance પર ગણાય છે? Ans: 5th day થી month-end વચ્ચેની lowest balance

👤 Operation of Account 🟡 Conceptual

Guardian ખાતું operate કરે — girl child 18 વર્ષની ના થાય ત્યાં સુધી
18 વર્ષ પછી ➜ girl child પોતે ખાતું operate કરી શકે (documents submit કરીને)
Probable Q: Girl child પોતે ખાતું ક્યારે operate કરી શકે? Ans: 18 વર્ષની ઉંમર પછી

🌳 Decision Tree — Premature Closure 🔴 High-Yield

(a) Death of account holder ➜ immediate closure, Form-2, death certificate

(b) Compassionate grounds — life-threatening disease / guardian's death ➜ after 5 years minimum, accounts office order
GroundTimingInterest
Death of account holderImmediate, any timeScheme rate till death; POSA rate death-to-closure
Compassionate closureOnly after 5 yearsFull scheme rate applicable
🧠 Mnemonic: "DC-5" — Death (anytime) vs Compassionate (after 5 years) — the two premature closure routes.
Probable Q: Compassionate ground પર premature closure ક્યારે માન્ય? Ans: ખાતું ખોલ્યાના 5 વર્ષ પૂરા થયા પછી જ

🏛️ Important Authorities 🟡

  • Central Government — notifies scheme under Sec 3A of 1873 Act; power to relax hardship (Para 11)।
  • Accounts Office (Post Office) — opens/closes accounts, credits interest, approves compassionate closure।
  • Guardian — operates account till girl child turns 18; opens account with birth certificate.
  • Municipal authority / Registrar of Births & Deaths / Indian Consulate — issues birth certificate for account opening.
  • Notary — attests marriage declaration for maturity-before-21 closure.

🚩 Important Exceptions 🟠

  • Max 2 accounts/family rule has exception for twins/triplets in 1st or 2nd birth order (affidavit + birth certificates required).
  • Exception does NOT apply if 1st birth order already produced 2+ surviving girls.
  • Compassionate premature closure NOT allowed before 5 years, even on medical/guardian-death grounds.
  • Withdrawal restricted strictly to actual fee/charges shown in admission proof — not a blanket 50% cash-out.
  • Marriage-based closure has a strict time window (1 month before to 3 months after) — outside this, not permitted.
  • Central Govt. may relax any hardship-causing provision (Para 11).
