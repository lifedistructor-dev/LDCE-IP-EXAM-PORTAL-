26.01 Introduction — IPPB Structure 🔴 VI▾

IPPB commercially launched 01.09.2018 with 650 branches/SOLs — each SOL further branches to facility IDs/access points and micro-ATMs (m-ATM). 100% Govt equity.

Per IPPB-DoP MoU, DoP acts as Corporate Business Correspondent (BC), providing middle-office & backend cash-management support at all IPPB access points (HO/SO/BO, GDS/Postmen).

🔴 Key fact: Postal Accounts Office, Delhi-110054 is the designated Nodal PAO for IPPB account settlement.

26.02 Transaction Types 🔴 VI▾

3 broad transaction categories:

TypeDescription
Cash ManagementCash deposits/withdrawals + AEPS payments to other banks; cash arranged via DoP treasuries at PO level
POSB-IPPB InteroperableSweep-in/Sweep-out between POSB schemes (SB, PPF, SSA, RD) and IPPB — funds move via digital transactions (NEFT/IMPS)
DoP Mail Business/Utilities PaymentsCustomer pays for mail/utility services via Direct Debit to IPPB a/c or QR code at POS

Sweep-out trigger: Since IPPB is a Payments Bank, RBI caps end-of-day balance — excess auto-sweeps to customer's POSB account.

26.04 Prefunding & Net Settlement 🔴 VI▾

Initial Deposit / Prefunding: IPPB maintains an agreed prefunding balance with DoP. DoP Nodal Office tracks withdrawals — recommended review every 30 minutes. If net cash deposit/withdrawal breaches 80% of prefunded balance, IPPB replenishes funds. Operated exclusively by PAO, Delhi.

Net Deposit (DoP owes IPPB)
Gross Deposit > Gross Withdrawal → PAO Delhi pays IPPB the difference
Net Withdrawal (IPPB owes DoP)
Gross Deposit < Gross Withdrawal → IPPB pays DoP the difference

Cash transactions in IPPB's Finacle shared with F&A application on T+1 basis (except non-rolled-out access points, handled manually by PAO from DDO data).

26.05 Sweep-in / Sweep-out Mechanics 🟠 FAQ▾

DoP operates 2 Current Accounts (Sweep-in, Sweep-out) in the name of Director of Accounts (Postal), Delhi, owned/operated solely by DoP.

Sweep-Out (excess IPPB balance → POSB): IPPB credits DAP-Delhi Sweep-out A/c on transaction/standing instruction → debited at EOD to meet RBI's Payments Bank limit → residual remitted to PAO Delhi Govt. Receipt A/c → balance = zero at EOD
↕
Sweep-In (POSB → IPPB): DAP-Delhi Sweep-in A/c debited on transaction/standing instruction → IPPB debits it at EOD → residual remitted back to PAO Delhi Govt. Receipt A/c → balance = zero at EOD

At day-start, DAP-Delhi places funds (per Directorate-fixed limit) into the Sweep-in Current A/c — total customer Sweep-in for the day is capped at this balance.

26.07 Role of PAOs (T+2 basis) & Nodal PAO Delhi 🔴 VI▾

Circle PAO duties (T+2 basis): reconcile Finacle cash MIS with CSI GL; send cash reconciliation report to Nodal PAO Delhi; tally IPPB GLs with e-Lekha; maintain LOT (List of Transactions) register; ensure no figures booked under PAO-Delhi-exclusive heads; ensure Mail Services/Utilities transactions transferred to Receipt MH-1201.

Nodal PAO Delhi — 22 responsibilities (key ones): fund Current Account per Directorate limit; net-settle cash transactions daily (or day-after for holidays); reconcile CSI F&A with IPPB Finacle data across all PAOs; prefund Sweep-in Current A/c; reconcile Sweep-in/Sweep-out remittances; claim mail-services balance from IPPB; reverse unreconciled DoP Finacle transactions; apportion IPPB commission among Circles; account MDR (Merchant Discount Rate); ensure IPPB Current A/c balance = zero at EOD; maintain guard file of sweep-funding approvals; submit fortnightly irregularity report to Directorate PA Wing & PBI Division; train all PAOs on IPPB reconciliation.

🔴 Key rule: IPPB Current Account balance must be ZERO at end of business hours — this is checked daily by Nodal PAO Delhi.
