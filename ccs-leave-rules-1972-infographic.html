Chapter I — Preliminary 🔴 VERY IMP

प्रारंभिक — Rules 1 to 6

📖 Definition / परिभाषा 🔴
English

The Central Civil Services (Leave) Rules, 1972 came into force on 1 June 1972. They apply to Government servants in civil services/posts connected with the affairs of the Union, subject to 11 listed exclusions (Railway servants, casual/daily-rated/part-time staff, workmen in industrial establishments, AIS members, etc.).

हिंदी

केंद्रीय सिविल सेवा (अवकाश) नियम, 1972 1 जून 1972 से लागू हुए। ये संघ के कार्यों से संबंधित सिविल सेवाओं/पदों पर लागू होते हैं, परंतु 11 सूचीबद्ध श्रेणियों (रेलवे कर्मचारी, आकस्मिक/दैनिक वेतनभोगी, औद्योगिक स्थापना कर्मी, अखिल भारतीय सेवा सदस्य आदि) को छोड़कर।

Probable Q: When did the CCS (Leave) Rules, 1972 come into force? Ans: 1 June 1972.

📜 Rules 4-6 — Transfer & Special Provisions 🟡
  • Rule 4: On temporary transfer to Railways/State Govt./foreign service within India → these rules continue to apply. On foreign service outside India or transfer to Armed Forces → rules apply only per terms of transfer.
  • Rule 5: Permanent Govt. servant transferred temporarily to a CCS(Leave)-Rules post → continues under old leave rules. If appointed substantively to a permanent post under these rules → becomes subject to these rules; leave at credit carried forward subject to Rule 26 max (300 days), old leave exhausted first.
  • Rule 6: If posted to an industrial establishment (Factories Act, 1948) → cash-equivalent of EL+HPL at credit granted (max 300 days) on ceasing to be governed by these rules; encashment on return capped so lifetime encashment doesn't exceed 300 days.

Chapter II — General Conditions 🔴 VERY IMP

सामान्य शर्तें — Rules 7 to 13

📜 Rule 7-8 — Right to Leave & Regulation 🔴
  • Rule 7: Leave cannot be claimed as of right. Competent authority may refuse/revoke leave when exigencies of public service require — but cannot change the kind of leave except at the servant's written request.
  • Rule 8: Claim to leave is regulated by rules in force at the time leave is applied for and granted (not when it accrued).
Probable Q: Can leave be claimed as a matter of right? Ans: No (Rule 7).
📜 Rule 9 — Effect of Dismissal/Removal/Resignation on Leave 🟠
  • Leave at credit lapses on dismissal/removal/resignation (except as provided in Rule 39 — encashment).
  • Exception: If servant resigns to join another post under Govt. of India (outside parent dept., application forwarded through proper channel) → leave does NOT lapse.
  • If reinstated on appeal/revision after dismissal/removal → prior service counts for leave.
  • Re-employed pensioner allowed to count past service for pension → also entitled to count former service towards leave.
Probable Q: Does leave lapse if a Government servant resigns to take up another post under Govt. of India through proper channel? Ans: No — exception under Rule 9(2).
📜 Rule 10-11 — Commutation & Combination 🟡
  • Rule 10: Leave may be commuted retrospectively into a different kind (that was due & admissible) at servant's request — not a matter of right. Request must be made within 30 days of rejoining duty after the leave.
  • Rule 11: Any kind of leave may be combined with/continued into any other kind, except Casual Leave (not "leave" under these rules) cannot be combined with any recognized leave.
Probable Q: Within how many days must a request for commutation of leave be made after rejoining duty? Ans: 30 days.
📜 Rule 12 — Max Continuous Leave 🔴

No Government servant shall be granted leave of any kind for a continuous period exceeding 5 years, unless the President determines otherwise in exceptional circumstances.

📜 Rule 13 — Acceptance of Service/Employment While on Leave 🟠
  • No employment elsewhere (incl. private practice) while on leave without prior sanction: President if abroad; Appointing Authority if in India.
  • Ordinarily not permitted to take other employment while on leave (other than LPR — Leave Preparatory to Retirement).
  • On LPR: private employment NOT permitted; but Public Sector Undertaking employment may be permitted (leave salary as per Rule 40).
  • If recalled to duty from LPR before retirement and agrees → unexpired leave from rejoining date is cancelled and can be encashed under Rule 39(2).
Probable Q: Who grants sanction for accepting employment abroad while on leave? Ans: The President.

📖 Definition — Rule 26 (Earned Leave) 🔴

English: Every Government servant (non-military, non-Vacation Dept.) is credited with Earned Leave in advance — two instalments of 15 days each on 1 January and 1 July. Maximum accumulation is 300 days (plus any days encashed with LTC). Unavailed joining time (up to 15 days) can be credited as EL under specified conditions.

हिंदी: प्रत्येक सरकारी कर्मचारी (सैन्य व अवकाश-विभाग को छोड़कर) को अग्रिम रूप से अर्जित अवकाश जमा किया जाता है — 1 जनवरी और 1 जुलाई को 15-15 दिनों की दो किश्तों में। अधिकतम संचय 300 दिन है।

Probable Q: How much Earned Leave is credited on 1 January and 1 July each year? Ans: 15 days each.

Probable Q: What is the maximum Earned Leave that can be granted at one time within India? Ans: 180 days.
📜 Rule 27 — Calculation of Earned Leave 🔴
  • Credited at 2½ days per completed calendar month of service likely to be rendered in that half-year.
  • In half-year of retirement/resignation → credit only up to actual date, at 2½ days/month.
  • On removal/dismissal → credit up to end of calendar month preceding the month of removal/dismissal.
  • On death in service → credit up to date of death.
  • If Extraordinary Leave/dies-non taken in a half-year → next half-year credit reduced by 1/10th of that period (max 15 days reduction).
📜 Rule 28 — Earned Leave for Vacation Departments 🟡
  • No EL if full vacation availed in a year.
  • If partial vacation availed → EL admissible proportionate to (30 days × unavailed vacation / full vacation).
  • If no vacation availed in a year → EL admissible under Rule 26 (normal formula).
  • Max accumulation still 300 days.
Probable Q: Is an employee in a Vacation Department entitled to Earned Leave if he avails the full vacation? Ans: No.
📖 Definition — Rule 29 (Half Pay Leave) 🔴

English: Half Pay Leave (HPL) account is credited in advance — two instalments of 10 days each on 1 January and 1 July, at the rate of 5/3 days per completed calendar month. May be granted on medical certificate or for private affairs. No fixed upper accumulation limit (unlike EL's 300-day cap).

हिंदी: अर्ध वेतन अवकाश (HPL) खाते में अग्रिम रूप से 1 जनवरी और 1 जुलाई को 10-10 दिनों की दो किश्तों में जमा होता है, दर 5/3 दिन प्रति पूर्ण कैलेंडर माह।

Probable Q: At what rate is Half Pay Leave credited per completed month? Ans: 5/3 days.
⚠️ Important Exceptions 🟡
  • HPL not granted to non-permanent/quasi-permanent employees unless authority believes they'll return to duty — except if declared completely & permanently incapacitated by Medical Authority.
  • Departmental leave under Rule 49 → 20 days HPL on completion of 12 months actual duty (special provision).
🧠 Mnemonic: "2.5 EL, 5/3 HPL, 15+15 & 10+10" — EL rate is 2.5 days/month (15+15 credit); HPL rate is 5/3 days/month (10+10 credit). "300 caps EL, no cap on HPL."

Commuted Leave, Leave Not Due & Extraordinary Leave 🔴 VERY IMP

रूपांतरित अवकाश, अननुज्ञात अवकाश एवं असाधारण अवकाश — Rules 30-33

📜 Rule 30 — Commuted Leave 🔴
  • Commuted leave (not exceeding half of HPL due) may be granted on medical certificate.
  • 🔴 Twice the amount of commuted leave is debited against HPL account.
  • Up to 180 days HPL may be commuted (without medical certificate) during entire service for an approved course of study certified in public interest.
  • If servant resigns/retires voluntarily without returning to duty after commuted leave → treated as HPL; difference in leave salary recovered (unless retirement due to ill-health or death).
Probable Q: How much HPL is debited when commuted leave is granted? Ans: Twice the amount of commuted leave taken.
📜 Rule 31 — Leave Not Due 🔴
  • Granted to permanent/quasi-permanent employees, max 360 days during entire service, on medical certificate.
  • Limited to HPL likely to be earned later; debited against future HPL earnings.
  • Also available to temporary employees suffering from TB, Leprosy, Cancer, or Mental Illness (max 360 days), subject to min. 1 year service + post likely to last till return + medical certificate.
  • Not available for Leave Preparatory to Retirement (LPR).
  • If resigns/retires voluntarily without returning → leave cancelled, leave salary recovered (except ill-health/death, or compulsory retirement under Pension Rule 48(1)(b) or FR 56(i)/(j)).
Probable Q: What is the maximum period of Leave Not Due admissible during entire service? Ans: 360 days.
📜 Rule 32 — Extraordinary Leave (EOL) 🔴

Granted when no other leave is admissible, or servant applies in writing for it despite other leave being due.

LimitCondition
3 monthsDefault max on one occasion (non-permanent/quasi-permanent)
6 monthsIf completed 1 year continuous service + medical certificate supports it (incl. the 3-month EOL)
18 monthsFor TB/Pleurisy/Leprosy/Cancer/Mental illness treatment (after 1 year continuous service)
24 monthsFor studies certified in public interest (after 3 years continuous service)
  • 🔴 For 24-month study leave → must execute a Bond (Form 6) to refund expenditure if not returning to duty or quitting within 3 years after return; Bond needs sureties from 2 permanent Govt. servants of comparable/higher status.
  • SC/ST candidates → EOL may be granted (relaxing sub-rule 2) for Pre-Examination Training Course by Head of Department.
  • Two spells of EOL, if separated only by other leave, are treated as one continuous spell for limit purposes.
  • Competent authority may commute retrospective unauthorized absence into EOL.
Probable Q: What is the maximum Extraordinary Leave admissible for pursuing studies certified as in public interest, and what condition applies? Ans: 24 months, after completing 3 years' continuous service, with a Bond (Form 6).

📜 Rule 33 — Leave to Probationer/Apprentice 🟡
  • Probationer entitled to leave only if he held the post substantively otherwise than on probation.
  • If services are to be terminated → leave granted cannot extend beyond sanctioned probation period or earlier termination date.
  • Apprentice: medical-certificate leave at half-pay leave salary, max 1 month per year of apprenticeship; also eligible for Extraordinary Leave under Rule 32.
🧠 Mnemonic: "3-6-18-24" → EOL limits in months: 3 (default), 6 (medical, 1 yr service), 18 (TB/Cancer/Leprosy/Mental), 24 (study, 3 yrs service + Bond). "Commuted = Half taken, Double debited."

📜 Rule 43 — Maternity Leave 🔴
  • Female Govt. servant (incl. apprentice) with fewer than 2 surviving children → 180 days from commencement.
  • Leave salary = pay drawn immediately before leave (reduced by ESI Act benefit, if applicable).
  • 45 days for miscarriage/abortion (irrespective of number of surviving children), on medical certificate — available once during entire service (post-1995 amendment).
  • May be combined with any other leave; leave of any kind (incl. commuted leave up to 60 days, and leave not due) up to 2 years may follow in continuation, without medical certificate requirement.
  • 🔴 Not debited against leave account.
Probable Q: What is the duration of Maternity Leave, and how many surviving children make a female Govt servant ineligible? Ans: 180 days; ineligible if she already has 2 or more surviving children.
📜 Rule 43-A / 43-AA — Paternity Leave 🟠
  • 43-A (Childbirth): Male Govt. servant with <2 surviving children → 15 days, from up to 15 days before to 6 months after delivery.
  • 43-AA (Adoption): Male servant with <2 surviving children, adopting child <1 year → 15 days within 6 months of valid adoption.
  • Both: full pay as leave salary; combinable with any other leave; not debited to leave account.
  • 🔴 If not availed within the specified window → lapses.
  • 🟡 Note: "Shall not normally be refused under any circumstances."
Probable Q: What happens if Paternity Leave is not availed within the specified period? Ans: It lapses.
📜 Rule 43-B — Child Adoption Leave (Female) 🟡
  • Female servant, <2 surviving children, valid adoption of child <1 year → 180 days immediately after adoption.
  • Full pay as leave salary; combinable with any leave; not debited to leave account.
  • May follow with further leave up to (1 year − age of adopted child on adoption date), incl. leave not due & commuted leave (max 60 days, no medical cert needed) — not admissible if she already has 2 surviving children.
📜 Rule 43-C — Child Care Leave (CCL) 🔴
  • Woman Govt. servant with minor children (<18 years) and no Earned Leave at credit → max 2 years (730 days) during entire service.
  • For up to 2 children; purposes: rearing, examination, sickness, or other needs.
  • Full pay as leave salary; combinable with any other leave; not debited to leave account.
  • May be availed in more than one spell.
  • Can be extended by up to 1 year (leave of kind due, incl. commuted leave ≤60 days, leave not due) without medical certificate, in continuation.
Probable Q: What is the maximum Child Care Leave admissible during entire service, and for how many children? Ans: 730 days (2 years), for up to 2 children.

Special Disability, Hospital, Seamen's & Departmental Leave 🟠 FREQUENT

विशेष असमर्थता, अस्पताल, नाविक एवं विभागीय अवकाश — Rules 44 to 49

📜 Rule 44 — Special Disability Leave (Intentional Injury) 🔴
  • For injury intentionally inflicted in/consequence of due performance of official duty or official position (permanent or temporary servant).
  • Disability must manifest within 3 months of the occurrence (relaxable if authority is satisfied on cause).
  • Max 24 months for any one disability (may be granted more than once if aggravated/reproduced, but overall cap remains 24 months per disability).
  • Counted as duty for pension; NOT debited to leave account (with a specific proviso exception).
  • Leave salary: first 120 days = EL rate; remaining period = HPL rate. Optional extension of EL-rate period by another 120 days (debited to HPL account).
  • Also applies to civil servants disabled via military service (discharged unfit, not fully incapacitated) & related certified disabilities.
Probable Q: Within how many months must a disability manifest itself to qualify for Special Disability Leave? Ans: 3 months.
📜 Rule 45 — Special Disability Leave (Accidental Injury) 🟡
  • Applies Rule 44 to injuries accidentally incurred in due performance of duty, or illness from a duty that increased risk beyond ordinary.
  • Disease-based disability must be certified as directly due to performance of the particular duty.
  • If contracted otherwise than with military force → must be "exceptional in character" per competent authority's opinion.
  • EL-rate leave under this rule capped at 120 days (no extension option, unlike Rule 44).
📜 Rule 46 — Hospital Leave 🟡
  • For Class IV servants, and Class III servants handling dangerous machinery/explosives/poisonous drugs/hazardous tasks — while under medical treatment for duty-related illness/injury.
  • Requires medical certificate from Authorized Medical Attendant.
  • Leave salary: first 120 days at EL rate; remainder at HPL rate.
  • Not debited to leave account; combinable with other leave; total combined leave capped at 28 months.
📜 Rule 47 — Seamen's Sick Leave 🟡
  • Officer/warrant officer/petty officer on Govt. vessel, sick/injured, on vessel or in hospital → leave on full pay, max 6 weeks.
  • Not granted if certified as malingering or ill-health due to drunkenness/self-indulgence/self-inflicted aggravation.
  • Disabled in exercise of duty → full pay leave up to 3 months, if: disability certified by Govt. Medical Officer; not due to own carelessness; vacancy not filled.
Probable Q: What is the maximum Seamen's Sick Leave on full pay for ordinary sickness, and for disability incurred on duty? Ans: 6 weeks (ordinary); 3 months (disabled in exercise of duty).
📜 Rule 49 — Departmental Leave 🟡
  • For Class III (field/recess duty in Survey of India) & Class IV staff, and seasonal P&T staff.
  • Granted only when services are temporarily not required (in addition to leave otherwise due).
  • Leave salary = 25% of pay admissible during EL, plus DA only.
  • Recess leave extendable up to 18 months total by higher authority; non-recess leave up to 6 months at a time, extendable to 1 year by Surveyor-General.
  • Does NOT count as duty; not debited to leave account; may be granted even when no leave is due; combinable with other leave.
Probable Q: What percentage of Earned-Leave-salary is paid during Departmental Leave? Ans: 25%.

1️⃣ Preliminary

  • 🔴 Came into force: 1 June 1972
  • 🟠 11 categories excluded (Rule 2) — Railway, casual/daily/part-time, contingency-paid, industrial workmen, work-charged, AIS, foreign-local, contract, special-law, FR/CSR-governed, limited deputation

2️⃣ General Conditions

  • 🔴 Leave NOT a right (Rule 7); kind can't change without written request
  • 🟠 Commutation request within 30 days of rejoining (Rule 10)
  • 🔴 Max continuous leave: 5 years (Rule 12)
  • 🟠 Employment abroad while on leave → President's sanction; in India → Appointing Authority's sanction (Rule 13)

3️⃣ Earned Leave & Half Pay Leave

  • 🔴 EL: 2.5 days/month; credited 15+15 (Jan/Jul); max 300 days; max 180 at a time in India
  • 🔴 HPL: 5/3 days/month; credited 10+10 (Jan/Jul); no accumulation cap
  • 🟡 Vacation Dept.: no EL if full vacation availed

4️⃣ Commuted / Not Due / Extraordinary Leave

  • 🔴 Commuted Leave: max half of HPL due; double debited to HPL
  • 🔴 Leave Not Due: max 360 days entire service
  • 🔴 EOL limits: 3 / 6 / 18 / 24 months (default/medical/TB-cancer-etc/study+Bond)

5️⃣ Maternity / Paternity / CCL

  • 🔴 Maternity: 180 days (<2 kids); 45 days miscarriage (once, any no. of kids)
  • 🟠 Paternity (birth/adopt): 15 days each, lapses if not availed in window
  • 🟡 Child Adoption Leave (female): 180 days
  • 🔴 Child Care Leave: 730 days (2 yrs), up to 2 children, needs zero EL at credit

6️⃣ Disability / Hospital / Departmental

  • 🔴 Special Disability (intentional): 3-month manifestation window; max 24 months; 120 days EL-rate + rest HPL-rate
  • 🟡 Special Disability (accidental): 120 days EL-rate cap, no extension
  • 🟡 Hospital Leave: 120 days EL-rate + rest HPL-rate; combined cap 28 months
  • 🟡 Seamen's Sick Leave: 6 weeks (sick) / 3 months (on-duty disability), full pay
  • 🟡 Departmental Leave: 25% of EL-salary; recess ext. up to 18 months
