📘 CHAPTER 1: CORE CEA RULES | मुख्य नियम

1️⃣ Definition & Rate of CEA परिभाषा एवं दर

🔴 Very Important
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

बाल शिक्षा भत्ता (CEA) केंद्र सरकार के कर्मचारियों को उनके बच्चों की शिक्षा हेतु दिया जाने वाला निश्चित मासिक भत्ता है, जो वास्तविक व्यय से जुड़ा नहीं है।

CEA is a fixed monthly reimbursement given to Central Government employees for their children's education, irrespective of actual expenditure.

CategoryRate (per month, per child)
Normal Child₹2250 (Fixed)
Divyaang (Disabled) Child₹4500 (Double, Fixed)
Hostel Subsidy (ceiling)₹6750 pm

📖 Governing Order

OM No.A-27012/02/2017-Estt.(AL) dated 16.07.2018 — consolidated instructions, effective from 1st July 2017 (post 7th CPC).

🧠 "2250 सामान्य, 4500 दिव्यांग, 6750 छात्रावास"

2️⃣ Eligible Children & Exceptions पात्र बच्चे एवं अपवाद

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

CEA केवल दो सबसे बड़े जीवित बच्चों के लिए देय है, कुछ अपवादों को छोड़कर।

CEA/Hostel Subsidy is claimable only for the two eldest surviving children, with specific exceptions.

🔀 Decision Tree: Is 3rd Child Eligible?
2nd child birth → Twins/Multiple?
→ YES →
All eligible for CEA
Sterilization operation failed?
→ YES →
1st child born after failure — eligible
❌ Simply not claimed CEA for 1st/2nd child → 3rd child still NOT eligible

⚠️ Important Exceptions

  • 2nd child birth results in twins/multiple births → all children covered.
  • Failure of sterilization operation → children from FIRST instance of such failure beyond 2-child norm are covered.
  • Non-claim for child 1/2 does NOT create eligibility for child 3.
🧠 "Twins ya Sterilization Fail = 3rd Bacha bhi Eligible, warna nahi"

4️⃣ Age Limit & Eligible Classes आयु सीमा एवं कक्षाएं

🔴 Very Important
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

सामान्य बच्चे के लिए अधिकतम आयु 20 वर्ष या 12वीं कक्षा पास, जो भी पहले हो; दिव्यांग बच्चे हेतु अधिकतम 22 वर्ष। न्यूनतम आयु कोई नहीं।

Normal child: max age 20 years OR till passing 12th, whichever earlier. Divyaang child: max age 22 years. No minimum age for any child.

CategoryMin AgeMax Age
Normal ChildNone20 yrs or passing 12th (earlier)
Divyaang ChildNone (5-yr rule removed w.e.f. 21.2.2012)22 yrs

📖 Eligible Classes

  • 2 classes before Class-I (i.e. Nursery/LKG/UKG) up to 12th standard.
  • Includes 11th/12th held by Junior Colleges or affiliated schools.
  • Initial 2 years of Diploma/Certificate course (Polytechnic/ITI/Engineering College) after passing 10th — ONLY if CEA/Hostel Subsidy not already availed for 11th/12th.
  • Correspondence/Distance Learning children — also eligible (subject to other conditions).
🧠 "20 Saal ya 12th Pass — Jo Pehle Ho; Divyaang ke liye 22"

5️⃣ Hostel Subsidy — Meaning & Conditions छात्रावास सहायिका

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

छात्रावास सहायिका उस व्यय को कहते हैं जो सरकारी कर्मचारी अपने बच्चे को अपने निवास से 50 किमी से अधिक दूरी पर स्थित आवासीय विद्यालय के छात्रावास में रखने पर वहन करता है।

Hostel Subsidy means expenses incurred by keeping a child in the hostel of a residential school/institution located beyond 50 km from residence.

Residential School
≥ 50 km
from Residence
→
Hostel Subsidy Eligible

📖 Important Rules

  • Reimbursable irrespective of transfer liability of the employee.
  • Applicable ONLY if child stays in hostel of the SAME residential school where studying (not any external hostel).
  • Components: fee for boarding + lodging (in addition to items in para 1(e) fee list).
  • Ceiling ₹6750 pm — whichever is LOWER of actual expenditure or ceiling is paid.
🧠 "50 KM se dur, Same School ka Hostel, Ceiling 6750"

6️⃣ Reimbursement Procedure & Certificate प्रतिपूर्ति प्रक्रिया एवं प्रमाण-पत्र

🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

CEA का दावा वित्तीय वर्ष पूर्ण होने के बाद, वर्ष में केवल एक बार किया जाता है। संस्था प्रमुख का प्रमाण-पत्र आवश्यक है।

CEA reimbursement is claimed only once in a financial year, after year completion, supported by Head of Institution's certificate.

📖 Important Rules

  • Certificate from Head of Institution confirming child studied during the academic year required.
  • If certificate unavailable → self-attested report card copy OR self-attested fee receipt(s)/e-receipts as alternative proof.
  • 50% of entitled amount can be claimed in 1st/2nd quarter; remaining in 3rd/4th quarter — OR full amount in last quarter.
  • 🔴 Frontloading (claiming full amount in advance in first quarter) is NOT permissible.
  • Duplicate receipts accepted (school-authenticated) if original misplaced; NOT accepted for non-school (private party) receipts.
  • Original school receipts need NOT be attested/countersigned/rubber-stamped.
Academic Year Completes
→
Obtain Certificate/Receipts
→
Claim Once per FY (quarterly allowed, no frontload)
🧠 "Saal mein 1 baar, Frontload Mana, Duplicate School Receipt Chalega"

8️⃣ Death, Retirement & Dismissal Situations मृत्यु, सेवानिवृत्ति एवं बर्खास्तगी की स्थिति

🔴 Very Important🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

सेवाकाल में मृत्यु होने पर, यदि पति/पत्नी सरकारी/PSU/अर्ध-सरकारी सेवा में नियोजित नहीं है, तो बच्चों को CEA देय रहता है।

If employee dies in service, CEA continues for children, provided the surviving spouse is not employed in Central/State Govt, Autonomous Body, PSU, or Semi-Govt organization.

9️⃣ Recognized Institution & Performance Link मान्यता प्राप्त संस्था एवं प्रदर्शन संबंध

🟡 Conceptual
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📌 Definition | परिभाषा

CEA का बच्चे के परीक्षा परिणाम से कोई संबंध नहीं है — फेल होने पर भी भत्ता जारी रहता है, परंतु उसी कक्षा में दूसरे स्कूल में दाखिला लेने पर भत्ता देय नहीं होता।

CEA is NOT linked to the child's performance/pass-fail result. However, if the child repeats the same class in a DIFFERENT school (after passing or mid-session), CEA is not reimbursable.

🔀 Decision: Same Class Repeated?
✅ Fails in SAME school, same class → CEA continues
❌ Passed/mid-session but joins SAME class in DIFFERENT school → CEA NOT reimbursable

🏛️ Recognized Institution Criteria

Must be recognized by Central/State Government, UT Administration, University, or a recognized educational authority having jurisdiction over the area — applies to nursery/LKG/UKG too.

🧠 "Fail OK hai Same School mein; Same Class Dusre School mein = No CEA"
❓ Probable Q: Is CEA linked to a child's academic performance? / क्या CEA बच्चे के शैक्षणिक प्रदर्शन से जुड़ा है?

🔟 Special Cases — Both Spouses / Nepal-Bhutan / Native Place विशेष स्थितियां

🟠 Frequently Asked
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📖 Important Rules

  • If both spouses are Government servants, only ONE of them can claim CEA/Hostel Subsidy for a given child.
  • CEA is payable for children of Central Govt employees who are citizens of Nepal/Bhutan (working for Govt. of India), even if children study in their native place — subject to certificate from Indian Mission confirming school recognition.
  • CEA/Hostel Subsidy CAN be claimed concurrently (both together) for eligible children.
🧠 "Dono Spouse Govt = Sirf Ek Claim Kare"

1️⃣1️⃣ Joining Time & Honorarium — Quick Facts यात्रा समय एवं पारिश्रमिक

📖 Joining Time Rules (CCS-JT Rules, 1979)

  • Govt servant treated ON DUTY during joining time; entitled to joining time pay (pay + DA/HRA/CCA as drawn at old post).
  • 🔴 Temporary employees with LESS THAN 3 years regular continuous service → get joining time but NOT joining time pay [Rule 4(4)].
  • Unutilized joining time (max 15 days, reduced by days actually availed) can be credited as Earned Leave [Rule 6(1)] — if servant joins new post without availing full joining time.
  • Joining time can be combined with vacation/regular leave of any kind — EXCEPT casual leave [Rule 6(2)].

1️⃣2️⃣ Special Allowance for Child Care (Women with Disability) दिव्यांग महिला हेतु विशेष बाल देखभाल भत्ता

🟡 Conceptual
विवरण देखें / View Details ▼

📖 Important Rules

  • Multiple births (twins) at FIRST child birth → NOT entitled to double rate for this special allowance.
  • Admissible for 2 years from birth of child, so long as woman employee does NOT have more than 2 surviving children.
  • If 3rd child born after 1st/2nd child dies before age 2 → allowance still admissible per above condition (surviving children ≤ 2).
🧠 "2 Saal tak, 2 Surviving Bacchon tak Hi"
